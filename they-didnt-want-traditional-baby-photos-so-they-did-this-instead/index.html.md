# They Didnt Want Traditional Baby Photos so They Did This Instead ...

Who hasn't fallen in love with the gorgeous maternity and newborn baby shots our friends have posted on Facebook (and we've secretly pinned on a "someday" board on Pinterest)? If you're looking for a new (and hilarious) twist on these traditional shots, read on - three couples have broken the internet with their own takes on the trend.

## 1. Jan and Chase Renegar's "Baby" Shoot

![Jan and Chase Renegar's "Baby" Shoot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/ph/c6kxasup_560x415.jpg)

... and their doggie-baby/Jack Russell terrier, Snuggles.

Atlanta couple Jan and Chase loved the work of a local newborn photographer so much, they scheduled their own shoot. The twist? They don't have a newborn... but they do have a darling doggie, who sat in for baby with hilarious (and oddly sweet) results. Aren't these photos adorable?
