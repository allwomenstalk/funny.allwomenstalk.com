# You Have to See IKEA's Silly but Sweet Love Manual ...

Let's face it: love can be hard, but according to the wizards at IKEA, it doesn't have to be. They've made us a love manual that's as easy to follow as the instructions for their fab furnishings, and it's truly divine. Let's have a look.

## 1. End the Argument

![End the Argument](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/mn/z20qy74356d065a590743414727441_600x358.jpg)

This simple lampshade will end the drama and you'll have a lovely piece of decor, too!

## 2. For the Inches-Challenged

![For the Inches-Challenged](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y6/d5/wpi6mfg456d065a654678545517810_600x346.jpg)

IKEA has the solution for any couple where one is much taller than the other.

## 3. Oh, Commitment!

![Oh, Commitment!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cc/wg/iohaifk356d065a665094570990979_600x275.jpg)

Don't give your commitment-phobic girlfriend a ring! Giver her a tasty treat instead. You'll win her heart and you'll spend a whole lot less.

## 4. The Cure for the Unwanted Kink

![The Cure for the Unwanted Kink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zm/em/zs6jm77w56d065a6e78ff048345286_600x341.jpg)

So maybe he's a little TOO into Fifty Shades. Trade in that king (kink?)-sized bed for two twins.

## 5. Martin!

![Martin!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gy/og/fjibfs8556d065a6d9b76858464934_600x366.jpg)

Replace the man who broke your heart with a chair bearing the same name.

## 6. Mend Your Broken Heart

![Mend Your Broken Heart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qg/sr/bfsozpeu56d065a6e1937836230943_600x206.jpg)

Sometimes it takes two to mend your broken heart.

Aren't these just so cute? Now I'm off to IKEA to find the love (or loveseat) of my life.
