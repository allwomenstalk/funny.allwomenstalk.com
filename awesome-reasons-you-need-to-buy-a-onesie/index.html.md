# 7 Awesome Reasons You Need to Buy a Onesie ...

So for the past 6 months, I have been telling my boyfriend that I want to buy a onesie. They just look so darn cute and comfy, and I saw Fergie and her hot hubby Josh Duhamel sporting blue and pink onesies in a magazine and I thought – now isn’t that adorable? Plus Miley debuted her twerking capabilities in one, and a slew of other celebs seem to love them! So here are 7 awesome reasons you need to buy a onesie!

## 1. It’s Super Comfy!

![It’s Super Comfy!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zt/rn/r7pvzu6p_530x699.jpg)

One of the best reasons to buy a onesie is due to the sheer comfort of one. My man bought me one for my birthday, and you would not BELIEVE the level of comfort these guys give you! It’s literally like being wrapped up in a hug, all the time. You’ll never want to take your onesie off because nothing else matches its fuzziness and warmth!

## 2. You Can Get It Customized

![You Can Get It Customized](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s1/tw/rctu0t96_460x503.jpg)

So the onesie I was given came from a place called The All In One Company, and I’m telling you, if you’re looking to buy a onesie, get it from here! The options are utterly endless. From the type of fabric to the colour of each part of your onesie, you can customize every little bit of it. You can even get it embroidered and get thumb holes in the sleeves! My guy picked polar fleece, and it is ridiculously soft!

## 3. It’ll Keep You Super Warm

![It’ll Keep You Super Warm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e6/gf/q13bn99l_600x903.jpg)

I am always, always cold. I have poor circulation, so my hands and feet feel like they’re frozen at any given time of the day. But my onesie solves all my problems! Seriously, I no longer need a heater in my room, instead I pull on my onesie (which also comes with a hood) and I instantly feel warmer. If you’re always cold like me, that’s a brilliant reason to get a onesie – to keep you warm!

## 4. Ryan Gosling Has One

![Ryan Gosling Has One](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/it/eak2461p_400x520.jpg)

He went on Ellen and they conducted their interview while wearing onesies. If Ryan Gosling says it's cool to wear a onesie, then it’s cool to wear a onesie.

## 5. It Even Comes with a Butt Flap

![It Even Comes with a Butt Flap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/ze/lqxa92uh_460x973.jpg)

You may think this is crazy, but it’s actually pretty awesome. There are tons of other cool additions you can get, but I love the fact that someone out there actually thought of this! Now you can buy a onesie and be warm no matter what you’re doing – literally.

## 6. You’ll Feel like a Kid Again

![You’ll Feel like a Kid Again](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o1/z5/tqlc71mp_481x724.jpg)

Another reason to wear a onesie is because it will make you feel like a kid again. It’s essentially a giant, warm, fuzzy jumpsuit. Which is just amazing.

## 7. It’s Practical

![It’s Practical](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ol/nx/f2dfwwqx_450x671.jpg)

All wonders of a onesie aside, hands done one of the best reasons to buy a onesie is because it’s actually practical. I never would have thought this to be true, but it is. Think about it – it covers up every part of you, so you don’t have to worry about your arms being cold, or your neck, and you get to lounge around at home in something comfortable. How could you not love that?

I love, love, love my onesie. I literally squealed with joy when my boyfriend gave it to me because it was something I wanted for such a long time. Now that I have it, I’m on a mission to get everyone else on board the onesie train, because it’s so amazing and comfortable! So tell me, would you want to buy a onesie?
