# 7 Silly Words of Wisdom That Will Inspire You to Succeed ...

When you're having a rough day, a few silly words of wisdom can completely alter your attitude. You don't want to go through life with a frown on your face and a lack of hope in your heart. You need to think positively, and remember that you'll succeed if you try your hardest. Here are a few silly words of wisdom to think about on the days you're not feeling enthusiastic or hopeful enough about your future:

## 1. Be Okay with Being Single

![Be Okay with Being Single](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/nx/yrn48uky_420x294.jpg)

You don't need a partner in order to live a fulfilled life. There are plenty of happy single women out there. Sure, it's nice to have someone to sleep next to, and to buy you nice things on holidays, but it's nicer to be happy with yourself. These are some of the silly words of wisdom you need to keep in mind if you're sad about being single.

## 2. Skip out on Some Sleep

![Skip out on Some Sleep](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c8/pt/w082rh7w_600x588.jpg)

It's important to get a good night's sleep, but don't be afraid to have some fun every once in a while. You don't want to miss out on awesome opportunities, because you chose to sleep rather than stay up for a few more hours. Don't let good times pass you by. Go out and have the time of your life while you're able to do so.

## 3. Just Be Happy

![Just Be Happy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/is/gh/ftu5xhxa_500x434.jpg)

Some people enjoy being miserable. They like to complain about how rough they have it, and get annoyed when they see others smiling. Don't be that type of person. Be the one that those people hate. You'll drive them crazy, but you'll be happier than ever.

## 4. Learning Takes Time

![Learning Takes Time](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ok/ck/drj2g0y8_600x600.jpg)

Don't feel too bad about your mistakes, because they're unavoidable. You're bound to make errors in life. It's impossible to be perfect, and you're never going to learn if you don't make those mistakes. One day you'll be old and wise, but for now, you get to be 'stupid' and do things that you might regret.

## 5. Don't Look behind You

![Don't Look behind You](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qr/lf/dqeh2ocz_529x384.jpg)

It's petty to gossip about others when they've done nothing to you. If people are talking about you behind your back, pay no attention to them. They aren't worth your time. Instead of dwelling on the things they say about you, prove them wrong by being successful in life.

## 6. Spread Happiness like Jam

![Spread Happiness like Jam](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h8/b5/s8vbo9bu_500x500.jpg)

If you're nice to others, you're going to feel better about yourself. Make sure you smile at everyone you see, even strangers, because that tiny action will make you happier. When you're generous, sweet, and just plain nice, you'll cause happiness in others, and end up being happier yourself.

## 7. Watch Your Back

![Watch Your Back](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ye/x3/w7i0nkml_404x317.jpg)

Unfortunately, you can't trust everyone you meet. Be careful about whom you choose to be friends with, because some people seem nice, but then end up doing something to hurt you. You don't want to be taken advantage of, especially by someone you once considered a friend. As long as you keep your eyes open, you should be fine.

If you want to be successful in life, you should follow these wise words of advice. No one is perfect, but you should always try to be the best person that you can be. As long as you show effort, your life will change for the better. What's your favorite inspirational quote?
