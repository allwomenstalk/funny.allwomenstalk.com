# 7 Cheeky Goals to Put on Your Realistic Bucket List ...

If you’re going to set goals for yourself, you might as well create a realistic bucket list. While you might be able to gain fame overnight or travel the world, you should put some items on the list that you can cross off easily. That way, you’ll feel better about your achievements. Here are some cheeky things to put on your realistic bucket list:

## 1. Go Get a Cat

![Go Get a Cat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/en/ir/ffnzp6e7_570x855.jpg)

You don’t even have to buy a cat (or a dog, if you’re not a feline person), because you might find one roaming around. If it doesn’t have a home, then you can be its savior and take it back to your place. This fits well on a realistic bucket list, because pets aren’t that difficult to attain. As long as you’re going to take good care of it, what’s stopping you from getting a furry friend?

## 2. Write a Letter

![Write a Letter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0h/ai/jp7745gc_600x600.jpg)

Instead of aiming to write a book, at least aim to write a handwritten letter to a loved one. Don’t you love the idea of scribbling down words to a friend or family member? You don’t have to use the phone to keep in touch with them. As long as your information isn’t urgent, you can be old fashioned for a day and write out a letter.

## 3. Sing KARAOKE

![Sing KARAOKE](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gv/wr/tb9wod6a_600x606.jpg)

Go to that karaoke bar down the block and get on stage. You know you want to, so what’s stopping you? Everyone’s dreamed of being a famous singer, but not everyone gets to live that life. Of course, there’s nothing stopping you from singing in front of a crowd. Take the opportunity to sing your favorite song, because you’ll regret it if you don’t.

## 4. Camp out

![Camp out](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wd/x7/y30w42z1_600x900.jpg)

If you're dying to see a concert or buy the newest electronic device, pitch a tent and wait in line to be the first customer. If you bring some friends along with you, then you could have a great time. Come on, you know you have to do it at least once. It'll be a great memory.

## 5. Live without Electronics

![Live without Electronics](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gb/mh/mohw5jui_600x600.jpg)

You always hear about people who turn off their cellphones and internet for days in order to see what it's like. You don't have to do this for a full week, or even a full day, but you should try it. Go half a day without your electronics, and see if life feels any different.

## 6. Splurge on Clothes

![Splurge on Clothes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n7/x7/0iajimjz_600x900.jpg)

You know you want to buy those boots that are ridiculously high priced. You shouldn't waste all of your paychecks on costly clothes, but you have to do at at least once. Get out that wallet and fish out your cash, because you're going to be the proud owner of a new outfit.

## 7. Survive Another Year

![Survive Another Year](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/0z/s4u4hc00_600x900.jpg)

This one sounds simple, but some days can be difficult to get through. If you manage to survive all of your toughest moments, then you’re a strong person! Be proud of every year, month, and day you get through, because it’s easy to give up hope.

There’s nothing stopping you from putting huge feats on your bucket list, but you might as well throw a few easy tasks on there. It'll make you feel better about yourself. What are some of the items on your bucket list?
