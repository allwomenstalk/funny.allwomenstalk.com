# 7 Quotes from Comedians That Will Give You Strength to Be Happy ...

There are quotes from comedians that will push you to succeed in life. Even though they find a way to make their speeches funny, it doesn't change the meaning behind them. There are some quotes from comedians that are profound, but still cause us to crack a smile. Here are a few important ones that you should keep in the back of your mind:

## 1. Ellen

![Ellen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fn/zj/mzkt6tcd_600x447.jpg)

Ellen loves to spread positively, and is one of the greatest role models out there. She has always been proud to be herself, even when the world was judgmental of her. This is one of the greatest quotes from comedians, because she's not just 'talking the talk.' She knows that her words are true, because she takes her own advice and is always true to herself.

## 2. Louis CK

![Louis CK](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rd/rv/yzovy1hj_600x387.jpg)

We spend so much time on our phones and laptops that we forget to acknowledge the beauty around us. If you're with your friends, put down your cell to talk to them. If you're walking down the street, pay attention to the scenery. You don't want to miss out on all of the amazing things going on around you. Keep your eyes open, and let them scan the area instead of letting them stay glued to a screen.

## 3. Jim Carrey

![Jim Carrey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qo/xj/gpfof7cz_486x337.jpg)

Everyone thinks that celebrities have the best lives, because they're rich and famous. However, money and popularity aren't everything. You make your own happiness. All you need are the right people surrounding you, and a job that you love. Everything else is secondary, but you might not realize that, because magazines and reality shows glamorize the lives of stars.

## 4. George Carlin

![George Carlin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bt/ld/bgfz51ir_600x279.jpg)

No matter what gender you are, you're going to have issues. It doesn't matter whether you're male or female. Either way, life is going to consist of ups and downs. There isn't as big of a difference between genders as everyone thinks. We're all human, after all. We have far more similarities than differences, so there's no reason to be confused by one another.

## 5. Robin Williams

![Robin Williams](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f4/uh/u1ej003b_403x275.jpg)

Instead of trying to hide your craziness, embrace it. It's what makes you an individual. It's what makes you beautiful. You don't want to push your madness away in order to fit in with the rest of society. Everyone else has little quirks that you may not see. Never try to change yourself, or let yourself lose your special qualities.

## 6. Bob Hope

![Bob Hope](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j1/l3/nz0x67cs_600x337.jpg)

Great things in life won't magically come to you. If you want to achieve something, you have to try your hardest to do so. You're not going to go anywhere in life by sitting around. You have to move and make things happen. Amazing things can occur if you simply try.

## 7. Ricky Gervais

![Ricky Gervais](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qw/dc/hpugnyfn_600x264.jpg)

While you shouldn't insult another's opinion, you certainly don't have to agree with it. Everyone has their own mind, which means that it's impossible for all of humanity to be in agreement on any given subject.

Comedians do so much more than tell funny stories. They comment on real life situations, and tell it like it is. Although they do a good job of making you laugh, they can also make you think. Who is your favorite comedian? Can you quote something they've said that you find inspirational?
