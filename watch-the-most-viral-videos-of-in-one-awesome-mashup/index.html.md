# Relive the 233 Most Viral Videos of 2014 in One Incredible Mashup

There’s something special about looking back at the videos that once took over our feeds. 2014 was a massive year for YouTube, filled with everything from charity challenges to heartwarming moments that just made us smile. This mashup is the perfect way to revisit that energy and see how much the internet has changed since then.
