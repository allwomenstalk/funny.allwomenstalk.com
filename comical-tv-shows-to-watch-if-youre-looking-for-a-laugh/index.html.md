# 7 Comical TV Shows to Watch if Youre Looking for a Laugh ...

There are many comical TV shows out there that will make you laugh if you're looking for something light-hearted to watch. With all of the tragic news stories and serious soap operas on TV, sometimes we need a bit of comic relief in our lives! I personally love to watch funny shows from time to time, so I have compiled a list of some of my favorite comical TV shows that never fail to raise a smile from me. .

## 1. Impractical Jokers

![Impractical Jokers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qt/kn/wgdxu386_600x450.jpg)

TruTV's Impractical Jokers is one of my favorite comical TV shows of all time! In essence, there are four friends who compete to embarrass each other by coming up with things that the others have to do or say. The guy that doesn't follow through with what the guys tell him to do or fails the most tasks has to do something extremely embarrassing at the end of the show as punishment. This show will definitely make you laugh!

## 2. Jackass

![Jackass](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j9/n0/j1vj0df1_600x400.jpg)

MTV's Jackass is similar to Impractical Jokers but much cruder. The guys on this show come up with different stunts or disgusting things that the others have to do. If you have a weak stomach, be careful as to which episodes of this show you choose to watch!

## 3. The Middle

![The Middle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/je/s7/dex8f4zt_600x480.jpg)

ABC's The Middle is a show about a middle class family and the ups and downs of their life. I love watching this show because I find I can relate to SO many of the things this family goes through, both good and bad. This has to be one of the most true-to-life and down-to-earth series on TV and life is portrayed in such a comical light!

## 4. Whose Line is It Anyway?

![Whose Line is It Anyway?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g8/ue/ky8kfuol_600x400.jpg)

ABC Family's Whose Line Is It Anyway? is a show where four people are given tasks that they have to act out on stage in front of a live audience. The actors are put on the spot to improvise a wide range of things from charades to singing a song on a random topic, and the stuff they come up with can be quite entertaining!

## 5. Colbert Report

![Colbert Report](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f8/ox/jggais8u_600x450.jpg)

Comedy Central's The Colbert Report is a politically-based satire associated with today's political issues. Stephen Colbert hosts the show and wittily comments on many controversial topics. If you are into politically-charged comedy, this may be a show for you to watch!

## 6. Fashion Police

![Fashion Police](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dk/xr/h0aea22m_600x450.jpg)

E's Fashion Police is a show dedicated to celebrities' fashion. Joan Rivers hosts this show and the comments that spew out of her mouth will either make you crack up or cringe. I personally love Joan River’s lack of filter because it makes the show that much more entertaining! If you are into the celebrity fashion scene and enjoy semi-crude humor, you may enjoy watching this show!

## 7. The Soup

![The Soup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m6/te/r4ao115r_600x400.jpg)

E's The Soup is a collection of the week's strange and comical clips on TV, narrated by Joel McHale. If you've ever watched something on live TV that made your jaw drop or was extremely embarrassing, chances are it wound up on that week's episode of The Soup.

There are many comical TV shows out there that are fun to watch, especially if you’re in the mood for something less-than-serious. What other TV shows do you watch when you are looking for a good laugh? Please share them in the comments section!
