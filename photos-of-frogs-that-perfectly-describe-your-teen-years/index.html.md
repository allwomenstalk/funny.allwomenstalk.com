# 13 Photos of Frogs That Perfectly Describe Your Teen Years ...

I was lying in bed sleepless a few nights ago and my mind started wandering to the weirdest places, so I found myself thinking what life as a frog must be like (yes, I'm deep like that); I turned on the PC and, tons of cute photos of frogs later, this silly article idea came to be. If you're looking for a useless (but fun) way to postpone whatever work you have to be doing right now, check out these adorable photos of frogs that perfectly describe your teen years!

## 1. The Dreamer

![The Dreamer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w6/wa/vop571aw_580x435.jpg)

"Is sound only sound if someone is there to hear it?" This must be one the most adorable photos of frogs I've ever seen (and I've seen plenty, trust me)! Isn't it the perfect portrayal of how we, as teenagers, used to spend most of the time daydreaming about boys, wonderful experiences awaiting us, boys, the meaning of life, and boys again?

## 2. Caught in the Act

![Caught in the Act](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/as/lk/fnnkxhbz_600x322.jpg)

We all must have had a boring, annoying teacher we absolutely hated, and he always was the one catching us daydreaming during class. Coincidence?

## 3. Guilty as Charged

![Guilty as Charged](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/bv/etgrvdku_600x400.jpg)

That look on your face when the teacher asked you what he just said!

## 4. Texting

![Texting](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/si/u1/ydripvhp_600x400.jpg)

"OMG! I can't believe this!!!" Do you remember that feeling when your crush texted you for the first time?

## 5. Playing It Cool

![Playing It Cool](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ow/xs/m1vihura_600x421.jpg)

And remember how you felt when he finally asked you out? You did your best to play it cool, but you were sooo excited! And the result was probably something painfully awkward for the both of you.

## 6. Oops!

![Oops!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rl/hg/gzoojbv9_600x450.jpg)

And then you said something so ridiculously stupid to him that you wished the ground would split in two in front of you so you could jump and hide in there forever and ever! "Lucky" me, I can't say I'm much better at these things now either.

## 7. Love is in the Air

![Love is in the Air](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ev/c7/wz1i3v21_600x400.jpg)

How about your first (and most probably clumsy) date with your crush? Doesn't this image perfectly describe it and isn't it one of the sweetest photos of frogs you've ever seen?

## 8. Learning the Hard Way

![Learning the Hard Way](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/br/ul/k9lalzaq_600x534.jpg)

However wonderful they may be, your teen years are also usually the time when you learn the hard way that not everyone around you is really your friend.

## 9. True Friendship

![True Friendship](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lz/rq/nn82fbub_600x388.jpg)

Fortunately, you do have your BFF to laugh it off with. And gossip with. And trash those mean girls with. "Haha, let me tell you what I heard about Claire, it's hilarious!"

## 10. We Will, We Will Rock You!

![We Will, We Will Rock You!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/js/j8/wfi184c8_486x321.jpg)

When your favorite band was in town, how many of you went to the venue hours before the concert started and stood in line for what seemed like forever, just to make sure you got a good spot? I don't think I'd have such patience now. I must be getting old.

## 11. I Want It All and I Want It Now!

![I Want It All and I Want It Now!](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/km/fc/uf0lhjts_600x450.jpg)

And when the concert was over, nothing could stand between you and the chance of meeting the lead singer!

## 12. Breaking the Law

![Breaking the Law](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sq/kx/xkvwrny5_600x450.jpg)

How many of you have sneaked out of the house to go party? Or sneaked back in way later than you should have been home, hoping nobody would notice it? You stealthy little ninja, you!

## 13. And Facing the Consequences

![And Facing the Consequences](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dy/ub/tenrk06v_600x400.jpg)

Surely it was not only once that your parents found out about your little escapades and grounded you. "You can take away my freedom, but my spirit will run free!"

You can go back to work now; you've reached the end of my list. I hope you enjoyed it as much as I liked putting it together. Which of these photos of frogs describe your teen years best?
