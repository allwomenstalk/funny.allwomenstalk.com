# 19 Ways to Show Your Love of Disney in Your Everyday Life ...

Are you looking for **ways to show your love of Disney**? ​I don’t know about you, but I. Love. Disney. I love the movies, the characters, the theme parks… everything! My boyfriend tells me I’m a Disney nerd, and I can’t disagree. I even worked at Disney World for a year.

​After I moved back home, it was sad. I loved being surrounded by the music (yes, even "It’s a Small World"), the happiness, the mouse! So, I started brainstorming ways for me to show my Disney side in a non-Disney world. Here are some of the best **ways to show your love of Disney.**

## 1. Clothes

![clothing, sleeve, fashion model, shoulder, t shirt,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v2/sf/fc0exr0a59bc0fcec37a5584993234_600x600.jpg)

I’m not sure how it happened, but 79% of my clothes are Disney themed. Tangled shirts, couples shirts, Disney festival shirts…but I’m not ashamed. Maybe I can’t wear them to work, but wearing one of my beloved Disney shirts when I go on a run or to the gym, makes it that much more enjoyable. Clothes are one of the best **ways to show your love of Disney.**

## 2. Wall Décor

![picture frame, font, art, home accessories, decor,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/iv/jef3ollh59bc40aeba1a6722400174_600x600.jpg)

Cover your walls with your love of Disney! There are canvases, tapestries, posters, vinyl, and of course, pictures from your own Disney vacations! There are countless quotes, songs, and characters you can use. Look on Pinterest for some inspo!

## 3. Jewelry

![jewellery, fashion accessory, hand, arm, finger,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wt/zu/hp8smcdn59bc128c5c228693800766_600x750.jpg)

Add some pixie dust to your bling! You can find Disney inspired earrings, bracelets, necklaces, watches, and rings almost everywhere.

## 4. Makeup

![pink, product, health & beauty, illustration, font,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m5/qo/zvo8zc1w59bc130f19880466850172_600x600.jpg)

Use your makeup to make a Disney inspired look. Or, look for some Disney inspired makeup! Different brands have released various Disney inspired makeup and brushes.

## 5. Bags

![fashion accessory, bag, handbag, product, coin purse,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/qo/y8krd2ww59bc15b69b7c9175257791_600x600.jpg)

Purses, backpacks, reusable shopping bags, overnight bags… the options are endless! Some high end brands have Disney inspired bags and I’ve gotten lucky finding Disney movie inspired reusable shopping bags in the Target dollar section or at my local grocery store.

## 6. Food

![cookies and crackers, chocolate, dessert, chocolate brownie, snack,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/n7/a0d2m1d159bc3e5bd86ef809230537_600x592.jpg)

You can get some of the yummiest (and Instagram-able!) food in Disney parks, but you can fill that void for Mickey shaped deliciousness in your own kitchen! There are Mickey shaped cookie cutters you can use to make pancakes, and of course, Mickey shaped waffle makers to make the ever loved Mickey waffles of Disney parks. There are also sprinkles, cereals, fruit snacks…let yourself be a kid again! Or, you can look on Pinterest for ideas on how to make Disney Parks inspired delicacies, like the tropical Dole Whip.

## 7. Shoes

![footwear, shoe, product, product, outdoor shoe,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/t1/qq98fk9s59bc19597d78b695514612_600x600.jpg)

Yes, Disney shoes are a real thing! Vans recently came out with a Toy Story line, and Keds has a Minnie Mouse inspired line! For my fellow runners, New Balance came out with an adorable RunDisney inspired line of running shoes! So fun! I’ve seen flats, and canvas shoes, and am sure there are more out there!

## 8. Undergarments

![footwear, shoe, pink, sock, fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i8/km/jzx0hlfc59bc3ea8ee3a9295530077_600x600.jpg)

So, for Christmas this past year, my Secret Santa got me THE greatest gift: Disney Socks. I love wearing them SO MUCH and am looking into getting more. I may have a Disney sock problem. In addition, when I saw Beauty and the Beast adult panties at Walmart, you can bet I bought a pair in a heartbeat.

## 9. Tattoos

![tattoo, joint, shoulder, temporary tattoo, arm,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ja/r4/wv2p6v3259bc3eea7452c686892942_600x600.jpg)

What better way to show your love of Disney than a tattoo?! From Mickey and Minnie to your favorite character, to the iconic castle, your Disney tattoo options are endless.

## 10. Bedding

![duvet cover, pink, bed sheet, textile, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jf/ng/bwvqfgv359bc3f4450109511261776_600x600.jpg)

Sure, it seems childish, BUT there's some great adult versions you can find out there too. Having Disney incorporated in your bedding is a great way to show your love without blasting it everywhere.

## 11. Car Accessories

![signage, font, sign, sticker, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zz/iy/hz3dyvbj59bc434a4360c186618498_600x600.jpg)

Window stickers, seat covers, or car decorations - Disney can truly be with you wherever you go when you have your car decked out.

## 12. Pins

![bag, white, pink, product, fashion accessory,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jw/tv/emex1o6y59bc44533009b303333183_600x600.jpg)

Pins are a great small accent to share your Disney love without being over the top. On your purse, a hat, or whatever you decide, pins are perfectly expressive.

## 13. Nails

![jewellery, nail, finger, fashion accessory, nail care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i3/yz/vi3lvww459bc40fcb79f5360213093_600x600.jpg)

Another cute and sweet accent to have Disney anywhere you are nail stickers. Buy them online or hit up the salon for some great Disney nail art.

## 14. Kitchenware

![table, tableware, coffee cup, porcelain, ceramic,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b0/ge/gp8zcnr359bc462901d09020605550_600x750.jpg)

From drink-ware to crock pots to pot holders, there are tons of Disney themed kitchen items to have a whole Disney inspired kitchen going on!

## 15. Bathroom Decorations

![product, technology, product design, product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x2/wn/qpxttjuu59bc468f7cd72028713691_600x600.jpg)

Disney towels are just the beginning of your Disney pride when you accompany it with a whole Disney bathroom! Toothbrush holders, bath mat, towels - the options are endless and not entirely over the top.

## 16. Office Supplies

![furniture, table, desk, chair, product design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yn/sd/asef6zgf59bc47bfeb39d145580158_600x600.jpg)

How cute (and fun) would your desk be with some adorable Disney office supplies?! You'll be the most festive and happy person in the office.

## 17. Phone Backgrounds

![pink, purple, girl, nail, finger,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dj/yf/jm3qtj7n59bc49df01d60489791205_600x749.jpg)

Everyone can appreciate a cute Disney background, and with the option of screenshotting, now your photo background choices are endless.

## 18. Workout Gear

![clothing, white, pink, sleeve, joint,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a6/ot/wr35blx659bc412c35c4c966870622_600x600.jpg)

Make exercise more fun by having your workout gear Disney inspired!

## 19. Phone Cases

![mobile phone, gadget, product, mobile phone accessories, font,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fp/vx/xsqegjpt59bc4ab27d705471632327_600x600.jpg)

Match your background to your case and have some awesome phone cases (cause you gotta have more than one to swap out) that show your true Disney love and pride!
