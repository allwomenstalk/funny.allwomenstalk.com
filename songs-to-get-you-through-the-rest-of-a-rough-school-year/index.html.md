# 7 Songs to Get You through the Rest of a Rough School Year ...

If you're under a lot of stress to complete assignments and give presentations, there are songs for a rough school year. Everyone hates school at some point, but it's an important part of life. As much as you want to graduate, you'll miss parts of your school experience once it's over. For now, here are songs for a rough school year to help you de-stress and laugh a little:

## 1. Anywhere else but Here by Simple Plan

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/DvnNYw)
The first words of the song are, "I'm staring at the clock. I'm wondering why I'm still here." If that doesn't explain your school experience, what will? Education is important, but sometimes you just want to get out of the building and into the sunlight. This is one of the best songs for a rough school year, because everyone can relate to it.

## 2. Campus by Vampire Weekend

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/vampire-weekend/270425072)
As frustrating as teachers and peers and homework can be, there are positive parts of school life. Think about all of the gorgeous people that are around you! You might meet the love of your life on campus, and Vampire Weekend sings about that possibility.

## 3. High School Never Ends by Bowling for Soup

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/high-school-never-ends-single/214757983)
If you're sick of all the drama that school entails, then this song will give you a reality check. Even when school is all over with, you'll still be stuck dealing with the same old issues. You'll still want your hair to look good, you'll still struggle to fit in, and you'll still deal with stress. It's something you have to get used to, so don't hate on teenagers too much when adults can act the same.

## 4. Chem 6A by Switchfoot

**On iTunes: **[itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/music-video/chem-6a/719642169)
This song proves that everyone has the same issues in school. You're not alone when you feel like you can't handle the stress. While you shouldn't resort to Sparknotes or watching the movie instead of reading the book, the temptation is always there.

## 5. College Kids by Relient K

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/two-lefts-dont-make-right-but/276318429)
College is expensive, difficult, and time consuming. Relient K sings about how hard college life can be, and that it's not for anyone. Of course, that doesn't mean that you shouldn't give it a try! You just have to figure out what you want from life and choose the path that's right for you.

## 6. Beauty School Dropout

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/grease-soundtrack-from-motion/3437853)
If you haven't seen Grease, you better give it a watch. This song talks about a girl who decided to drop out of high school, and regrets it. It shows just how important education can be. Even if you feel like quitting, you have the strength to get through it all. Keep going, because graduation will come quicker than you think.

## 7. Mr Watson by Kesha

**On iTunes:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/mr-watson-single/677618921)
If you're unable to smile, because of all of the school stress, this song will make you laugh. It's about a girl who has a crush on her teacher, and tries to hook up with him. While this certainly isn't something that you should attempt, it's funny to think about.

Despite the content of these songs, you should realize how lucky you are to be enrolled in school! Education is incredibly important, and you should appreciate it as much as you can. What's your favorite class that you're currently taking?
