# 7 Bowling for Soup Songs That Guarantee a Laugh ...

If you’ve heard any Bowling for Soup songs, you know how humorous their lyrics are. They can make any topic funny. Whether you're a huge fan or have never heard a word from them, here are some of the funniest Bowling for Soup songs you should listen to today:

## 1. High School Never Ends

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/high-school-never-ends-single/214757983)

One of the best Bowling for Soup songs is called “High School Never Ends.” It talks about how life after high school is pretty similar to high school itself. It compares our favorite celebrities to prom queens and class clowns. If you’re worried about your reputation, this song will help you deal with the stress of life. Everyone can relate to it in some way, which is why it’s such an amazing song.

## 2. Here’s Your Freakin’ Song

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/fishin-for-woos/426764240)

This is a song about not wanting to write a love song. If you’re a lyricist that’s in a relationship, you know the pressure that you’ll have to write a song for your partner. You write about everything else, so why wouldn’t you write about them? They’ll be angry if you don’t, so you’re forced into doing it, even though you have nothing nice to say. That’s the basis of this song, and it’s pretty darn hilarious.

## 3. Friendly Goodbye

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/great-burrito-extortion-case/202603021)

Have you ever wanted to kick someone out of your life in the nicest way possible? Then this is the song for you! Of course, the lyrics are far from friendly. They try to put a nice spin on deleting someone from your life, but it’s impossible to do. No matter what you say, it’s going to sound horrible, so you might as well be blunt.

## 4. A Really Cool Dance Song

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/sorry-for-partyin/333609697)

This is a song making fun of the music industry. Since dance songs have become so popular, Bowling for Soup decided to make one of their own. If you like dance songs, you'll love this. If you hate dance songs, you'll also love this.

## 5. Normal Chicks

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/lunch-drunk-love/688108173)

If you’ve ever felt insecure about yourself, this song could raise your confidence. It talks about how you don’t have to have the body and face of a model in order to be attractive. There are plenty of girls that don’t think that they’re good enough, but of course they are! This song will push you to believe how worthy you are, all while making you laugh.

## 6. Girl All the Bad Guys Want

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://goo.gl/VvjBxp)

You might’ve heard this one before, since it’s one of the more popular songs. It’s about a guy who likes a girl that is nothing like him. She prefers her men with mustaches and mullets, but he’s stuck driving a moped. If you’ve ever liked someone that’s in a totally different league as you, you’ll enjoy the humor in this one.

## 7. Next Ex-girlfriend

**On iTunes at:** [itunes.apple.com](https://music.apple.com/us/album/a-hangover-you-dont-deserve/271371083)

This song talks about how messy relationships can be. Whether it’s a serious one, or a fling, someone usually ends up hurt. This song simply talks about how he doesn’t want to be bothered with love or romance. Even if you’re a sentimentalist, there was a time in your life when you could relate to this.

Bowling for Soup is an awesome band with dozens of songs to brighten your day. What's your favorite song of theirs?
