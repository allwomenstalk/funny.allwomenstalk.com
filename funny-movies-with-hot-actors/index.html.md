# 11 Funny Movies with Hot Actors ...

There are funny movies with [hot actors](https://movies.allwomenstalk.com/top-actors-with-the-hottest-bodies/) that you should see with your best gal pals. While it's always fun to laugh, it's even more fun to laugh whilst admiring the male body. There are tons of gorgeous guys out there, and you might as well appreciate their beauty. Here are some funny movies with hot actors that you'll love seeing on your screen:

## 1. Girl Most Likely

Do you have a thing for Darren Criss? How about a girl crush on Kristin Wiig? Well, they'll both make you smile in this must see film. This is one of the funny movies with hot actors that will simultaneously make you laugh and drool.

## 2. That Awkward Moment

Zac Efron has gotten hotter as the years have gone by, and his costars aren't bad to look at either. This movie shows the workings of three different types of relationships. No matter what kind of guy you're attracted to, you'll find some eye candy in this movie.

## 3. The Proposal

Ryan Reynolds is gorgeous, so you'll love looking at him without his shirt on. Plus, the story line of the film isn't bad either. It's a cute romantic comedy that anyone can enjoy.

## 4. A Cinderella Story

Chad Michael Murray used to be one of the hottest actors around. Although he's not talked about often anymore, it doesn't change the fact that he was adorable. Even though this movie is meant for teens, it's worth the watch for any age group.

## 5. She's the Man

Channing Tatum has been a looker his entire life. Although this film is almost a decade old, he appears just as fit and sexy as he does now. Some things never change.

## 6. 13 Going on 30

You might be used to seeing Mark Ruffalo play the Hulk, but he can be a romantic when he wants to be. Of course, he's not the only adorable guy that appears on screen. There are plenty of shirtless scenes with another hottie.

## 7. New York Minute

How long has it been since you've watched a Mary-Kate and Ashley film? You know that you loved them, so why not relive your childhood by watching this film? The twins have amazing taste in boys, and the band members of Simple Plan even appear.

## 8. Love Actually

Martin Freeman, Colin Firth, and Andrew Lincoln all appear in this romantic comedy. How much more sexiness could you possibly fit on one screen?

## 9. Win a Date with Tad Hamilton

Josh Duhamel and Topher Grace both star in this hilarious film. If you've never watched it, you should do so immediately. It's a cute story that will make you laugh, and make you fall in love with at least one of the two boys.

## 10. John Tucker Must Die

John Tucker can get any girl he wants, and there's a reason for that. He's absolutely gorgeous. Of course, if you prefer Penn Badgley, he's around to gawk at, too.

## 11. The House Bunny

Tyson Ritter, the lead singer of The All-American Rejects, plays Emma Stone's love interest in this film. They seem like an odd couple, but they're actually adorable together. If you're in the mood to see a cutesy couple, this is the film for you.

The next time you're searching for a film to watch, put on one of these. If they aren't able to make you laugh, the men will at least keep you entertained. Who do you consider the hottest actor?
