# 17 Struggles All Short Girls Can Relate to in Life ...

Tall girls - and heck, even normal-height girls - just don't understand the daily struggles of short girls. The advantages are few (plane travel is more comfortable, for one) but the struggle is real. Here are a few of the short girl problems that need to be solved, stat.

## 1. Capris, Please

![Capris, Please](https://img.allw.mn/content/av/a7/n0plcmvs572e47f1d9354265921138_300x200.gif)

Capris are on-trend right now, so you'd think most labels would have a cropped pant that flatters short girls, but no. Instead of hitting us mid-calf, they're ankle-length or longer. Why?

## 2. Mirror, Mirror

![Mirror, Mirror](https://img.allw.mn/content/b8/dy/twy3vscm572e4862ec9e1523748476_500x282.gif)

The mirrors in public restrooms are usually okay, but sometimes it seems they cater specifically to women who only want to see themselves only from the eyebrows up.

## 3. Shopping the Shelves

![Shopping the Shelves](https://img.allw.mn/content/ji/s5/nxo57rf0572e4d845f1c4708284418_242x322.gif)

If it's on the top shelf at the grocery store, you must not need it.

## 4. Long Sleeves

![Long Sleeves](https://img.allw.mn/content/o3/e0/slhbhoky572e49b293e6d459653684_500x275.gif)

Long sleeves really ARE long for anyone under five-two. You're probably sick of rolling your cuffs. I know I am!

## 5. Two Hug Choices

![Two Hug Choices](https://img.allw.mn/content/oe/zh/hq57xb94572e4a4f98e30493951748_207x240.gif)

If you're hugging your sigoth, you'll end up in his armpit. If you're hugging your bestie, you'll end up with her boobs in your face.

## 6. The Chairs

![The Chairs](https://img.allw.mn/content/y3/o2/nuivwppz572e4a3d55056766604075_400x263.gif)

Why can't someone invent an office chair for shorter people, so our feet can actually touch the ground?

## 7. Keep up!

![Keep up!](https://img.allw.mn/content/l7/n6/o33uep4e572e4aeb3ba7d802548554_500x200.gif)

When you're walking - or heaven forbid, running - with your friends, you'll have to work a lot harder, or fall behind. Boo.

## 8. Washing Machine

![Washing Machine](https://img.allw.mn/content/yo/gj/mtju80ez572e4b5832cb7383253239_500x245.gif)

I'm so glad someone finally came up with the front-loading washing machine, because I was so tired of having to practically fall into my old top-loader to get all of my laundry out!

## 9. The Comments

![The Comments](https://img.allw.mn/content/bd/w2/os0zuogh572e4ab17aef8838142942_500x283.gif)

Call me "cute" one more time. Go on. I dare you.

## 10. The Questions

![The Questions](https://img.allw.mn/content/qc/n3/xz0kfq9c572e4adc58ac5168307425_500x276.gif)

Or, you're such a comedian! Yes, I DO know how short I am! Thanks for asking.

## 11. The Pool

![The Pool](https://img.allw.mn/content/eq/ef/odqbuu9m572e4b7fb6b66607623423_500x272.gif)

I guess this could be considered a plus, but when you're a short girl, the entire pool is the "deep end."

## 12. The Movies

![The Movies](https://img.allw.mn/content/l9/sb/w0ebwayy572e4c89cca37438902039_500x213.gif)

Ugh, is there anything worse than sitting behind a row of tall people at the movies?

## 13. The Seat

![The Seat](https://img.allw.mn/content/or/cr/oqh25w23572e4cf668b75408668246_500x281.gif)

Whenever anyone drives my car, I have the pleasure of having to readjust my car seat. On the plus side, they have to move it in the first place.

## 14. Roller Coaster

![Roller Coaster](https://img.allw.mn/content/rj/zx/gejphr00572e4cadaa15f441778619_500x260.gif)

There have admittedly been a couple of times I was seriously worried that after waiting an hour in line for the roller coaster, I'd be too short to make it on the ride.

## 15. Career Choices

![Career Choices](https://img.allw.mn/content/c3/v7/oa8caz2f572e4bcef141c270000903_500x281.gif)

Basketball player? No. Horse jockey? Sure!

## 16. Bedtime

![Bedtime](https://img.allw.mn/content/vi/n9/ubz7aq3f572e4d1e53180447916612_500x281.gif)

Why are the beds in some hotels so far off the ground? And why don't they at least have a little stool next to them so you don't have to get a running start at bedtime?

## 17. The Hump

![The Hump](https://img.allw.mn/content/jr/yg/y9csbuzk572e4dd80c3a1077572351_500x270.gif)

It's so not fair! Ride in a car with four or more friends, and guess who gets relegated to the middle spot in the back seat with that stupid awkward hump? No fair!
