# Which Horror Movie Monster You Are According to Your Zodiac Sign ...

Ever wondered **which horror movie monster you are according to your zodiac sign**? Horror movies - they are something that most people love, and equally something that most people are terrified of! We all love to get together with friends or a partner and hide behind our hands from the safety of the couch, but have you ever turned the tables and thought about which role you would take if required to be an iconic villain? Isn’t there a famous saying along the lines of we all have the capacity to be monstrous if pushed to the edge, well, now its time to imagine it! Here is **which horror movie monster you are according to your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Gemini

You are extremely intelligent and very manipulative when you want to be, so it makes sense that your closest horror villain match is definitely The Joker from Batman!

![zombie, fictional character, supervillain, blood, joker,](https://img.allw.mn/content/kc/ma/kvrlyys85b1baebc1692b141493965_500x268.gif)

## 2. Cancer

No matter what you do in life, your main concern is always your family, and the very worst interpretation of this is without a doubt being a member of the infamous clan in The Texas Chain Saw Massacre!

![sky, phenomenon, tree, film, darkness,](https://img.allw.mn/content/lp/cq/sz5s6std5b1baebc3bb80349177652_480x223.gif)

## 3. Leo

You are charming, charismatic and suave. You can pretty much convince anyone to do anything. Who does that sound like to you? Yep, that’s right, none other than Dracula!

![face, nose, head, aggression, darkness,](https://img.allw.mn/content/sf/lo/uvhn7taa5b1baebce81ec342053826_500x250.gif)

## 4. Virgo

You are super sweet and innocent on the outside, but on the inside, you have a mean streak if people rub you the wrong way, kind of like the ultimate momma’s boy Norman Bates from Psycho!

![person, man, black and white, monochrome photography, photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/kz/ia/npl2ueeb5b1baebe8aac6792435089_500x275.gif)

## 5. Libra

You are an underdog, somebody that isn’t always taken seriously but can make a big impact when the time is right. Kind of like the unassuming but deadly doll Chuckie!

![child, toddler, human hair color, toy, doll,](https://img.allw.mn/content/n8/v7/id5fm0bl5b1baebe4b289472917916_500x281.gif)

## 6. Scorpio

You are definitely misunderstood and sometimes feel like you can’t express your feeling to those around you, just like our favourite classic movie monster. You know, the one created by Frankenstein!

![black, black and white, eyewear, monochrome photography, photography,](https://img.allw.mn/content/hf/pc/efvr0igz5b1baebecc974995951513_500x250.gif)

## 7. Sagittarius

You are very detail oriented and a great thinker, much like the unique but harrowing John Doe of the unforgettable horror thriller Se7en!

![head, chin, forehead, human,](https://img.allw.mn/content/pc/lz/fi8ifzkk5b1baebf13138472710483_268x153.gif)

## 8. Capricorn

Highly intelligent, hyper-aware of your place in the world, confident in your own decisions, your best traits when written down on paper definitely sound like the one and only Hannibal Lecter!

![face, person, nose, forehead, chin,](https://img.allw.mn/content/q0/cx/bgm4pidm5b1baebf2abbb446934318_500x265.gif)

## 9. Aquarius

![Aquarius](https://img.allw.mn/content/j9/hy/vpk0aszp5b1baec0c575c920211340_500x247.gif)

You are creative but your most noticeable quality is your amazing sense of humour, and which horror monster is known for his razor-sharp wit? Why, Freddy Krueger of course!

## 10. Pisces

When it comes to your relationship with your best friend, you are ride or die. So much so, in fact, that you could definitely be compared to Ghostface from Scream!

![black, face, head, darkness, emotion,](https://img.allw.mn/content/m6/j3/h7l4wtqr5b1baec1784d2163422155_540x223.gif)

## 11. Aries

You are fiery and impulsive, some might say a natural born predator, so there is no other monster that fits quite as well as the iconic Xenomorph from the Alien franchise!

![water, sky, underwater, marine biology, organism,](https://img.allw.mn/content/cz/f7/ir5r2cvx5b1baec16718f470754161_500x209.gif)

## 12. Taurus

You are extremely stubborn at times, and can hold a grudge better than anyone, much like the long dead but long revenge hunting Jason Voorhees from Friday The 13th!
