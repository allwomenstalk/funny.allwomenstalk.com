# Yuk! 12 Stomach Churning Kisses We Cant Stand ...

The closeness, the butterflies, the fireworks when your tongues intertwine...kissing is truly magical, right? Well, yes of course but where there's good, there's also evil and some kisses are downright disgusting! Have you experienced any of these awful kisses?

## 1. The "I'm Looking for Lost Treasure in Your Mouth" Kiss

![The "I'm Looking for Lost Treasure in Your Mouth" Kiss](https://img.allw.mn/content/vh/a2/a52rhkt456d4bf315bf47772838174_427x200.gif)

You know the kind where the other person just sticks his/her tongue deep (and I mean DEEP) inside your mouth? HELP!

## 2. The "I'm a Puppy" Kiss

![The "I'm a Puppy" Kiss](https://img.allw.mn/content/la/j0/yc908hij56d4bf324b73e838645660_500x230.gif)

You know what I'm talking about. The very wet, very sloppy kiss that makes you feel like you just washed your face...with someone else's saliva. Ugh!

## 3. The "I'm Going to Eat You" Kiss

![The "I'm Going to Eat You" Kiss](https://img.allw.mn/content/k6/zc/khum034u56d4bf3447f6c319778100_500x279.gif)

This is where the other person opens his mouth so wide, that you feel like you're being eaten alive.

## 4. The "closed Mouth" Kiss

![The "closed Mouth" Kiss](https://img.allw.mn/content/mw/sz/a93l54ou56d4bf3542044548701042_400x223.gif)

The opposite of #3, where your partner just refuses to open his/her mouth. Let me in!

## 5. The "Eye Opener"

![The "Eye Opener"](https://img.allw.mn/content/tw/i7/mzvecdcp56d4bf3681627182366379_500x241.gif)

Unless you're doing it together for fun, kissing with your eyes open is just creepy!

## 6. The "Biter"

![The "Biter"](https://img.allw.mn/content/hg/bn/ohk78bxw56d4bf36cbf68362279554_500x375.gif)

Okay, a gentle nip in the heat of the moment is fine but anything harder than that is just better in theory. Seriously, leave it to the vampire movies.

## 7. The "It Feels like I'm Kissing a Dead Person" Kiss

![The "It Feels like I'm Kissing a Dead Person" Kiss](https://img.allw.mn/content/cl/gf/jgsp8xxt56d4bf3b5b311757152132_299x213.gif)

You know where you're doing all the work and the other person just sits there with his mouth open? What a turn-off!

## 8. The "accidental Kiss"

![The "accidental Kiss"](https://img.allw.mn/content/cv/tm/l0flib9256d4bf3c64eae983800599_379x215.gif)

What's the accidental kiss, you ask? Well you know, when you lean in to do the "hug and kiss on the cheek" routine with someone but you’re not quite sure if he/she is going for your right cheek or left so you sort of hover mid-air until your lips meet and before you realize it….smack! You’ve just accidentally kissed someone…on the mouth! The worst is when it happens with an acquaintance. KILL ME!

## 9. The "Pecker"

[https://media.giphy.com/media/8lz87DaYAURSo/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/8lz87DaYAURSo/giphy.gif)
Yes, pecks are cute but not when it's the only thing you do...over and over. You're not a bird!

## 10. The "vacuum"

![The "vacuum"](https://img.allw.mn/content/fx/xy/vyfr75c356d4bf3dd9200003982092_260x260.gif)

Kissing does not mean trying to suck the life out of someone. Besides, suffocating your partner is NOT romantic!

## 11. The "I Forgot My Lip Balm" Kiss

![The "I Forgot My Lip Balm" Kiss](https://img.allw.mn/content/js/bt/d66jfok256d4c08b12bfd695396407_500x267.gif)

Okay, maybe it's not the worst thing on this list, but chapped lips are not fun!

## 12. The "What's That I Smell?" Kiss

![The "What's That I Smell?" Kiss](https://img.allw.mn/content/n3/c5/dc71gfbf56d4bf3dcecaa819074757_500x208.gif)
