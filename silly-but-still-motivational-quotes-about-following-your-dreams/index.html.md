# 7 Silly but Still Motivational Quotes about following Your Dreams ...

Since you were a child, you've been told to follow your dreams. If you're sick of hearing the same old advice, no matter how true it is, you'll enjoy the following quotes that poke fun at the idea of following your dreams. However, they'll still manage to get you motivated to reach your goals. Here are a few silly but motivational quotes about following your dreams:

## 1. Making Millions

![Agricultural engineering,human action,text,person,ecosystem,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/qz/xxc47_agricultural-engineering_human-action_text_person_ecosystem_540x427.jpg)

Think about all of the ridiculous things you've seen throughout your life. With so many crazy inventions and careers, no one should laugh at you for wanting to accomplish something that sounds silly. If they do, then ignore them and their negativity. You're the one who will benefit from following your dreams while they'll be the one stuck living a mediocre life for the rest of their days.

## 2. Never Skip a Nap

![UNHAS Snoopy,mammal,cartoon,biology,flightless bird,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dc/ru/g0mwj_unhas-snoopy_mammal_cartoon_biology_flightless-bird_600x414.jpg)

Don't laugh just yet, because dreams can actually help you out a lot in life. If you want to be a writer or a painter, you could see something in a dream that'll inspire your next project. Taking a nap isn't always a sign of laziness. Sometimes it's a way of making your brain more active.

## 3. Always Ask Questions

![text,cartoon,font,brand,presentation,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xr/lp/cu02d_text_cartoon_font_brand_presentation_420x294.jpg)

There's some truth to this silly quote. You shouldn't blindly follow your dreams without thinking things through first. You need to ask questions. Figure out why you want to do what you want to do and research the necessary steps to getting there. Those questions are of the utmost importance.

## 4. Now Instead of Never

![text,font,brand,presentation,It's,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ly/zw/lfsbg_text_font_brand_presentation_its_403x403.jpg)

It's nice to think that you're never too old to follow your dreams, but sometimes it isn't actually the case. That's why you need to try to accomplish as much as you can right here and now, while you're still young. Sure, you can try again when you're older, but you might as well use your youth to your advantage. Reach for the stars.

## 5. Downside of Dreams

![text,font,handwriting,line,brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o0/d0/aemk8_text_font_handwriting_line_brand_403x275.jpg)

Following your dreams certainly isn't an easy task. It can take a lot of hard work, which is why you need to be willing to get down and dirty before you reach your goal. Nothing worth having comes easily. That means that standing on a few cacti will be worth it once you're finally successful and living the life you've always dreamed about having.

## 6. Risky Reality

![Woman,El Komander,text,font,product,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i6/g2/jx0ih_woman_el-komander_text_font_product_420x294.jpg)

Some dreams just aren't meant to turn into reality. If you and your boyfriend dream about becoming the next Bonnie and Clyde, that's a fantasy you should keep to yourselves. Sure, a life on the run seems fun, but it's not one you want to live. Be careful about which dreams you choose to follow.

## 7. Save the Stories

![Bombay Duck,text,font,line,brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kb/r2/fbrlc_bombay-duck_text_font_line_brand_420x294.jpg)

Even if you fail at becoming a famous singer, your attempts at success will make for a great story one day. Your life might not work out exactly the way you want it to, but it's better to know that you tried. If you spend your days sitting on your couch without putting in any effort, you'll always reprimand yourself for not doing your best. You don't want to have to look back at your past with regrets.

It's important to keep following your dreams, no matter how many times you fail. Eventually, everything will work out and you'll be thankful that you kept trying. What's your biggest dream in life?
