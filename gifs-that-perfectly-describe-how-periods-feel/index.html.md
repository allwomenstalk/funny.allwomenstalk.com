# These Great Gifs Perfectly Describe How Periods Feel ...

Unfortunately, you already know how periods feel, but seeing some GIFs that understand your pain can help you cheer up during that time of the month. No woman wants to spend a week in pain, but it's something we all have to deal with. We may not be able to stop our bodies from torturing us, but we can at least laugh about it all. Here are some silly GIFs that show exactly how periods feel:

## 1. Cravings

![Cravings](https://img.allw.mn/content/cj/ir/qcm4q4h3_500x270.gif)

You're going to crave certain foods during your time of the month. If you're on a diet or trying to eat healthy, your period will urge you to have a few cheat days. Your body wants that chocolate and isn't going to stop bothering you until it gets it. Being hungry all day, every day is how periods feel, and there's nothing we can do to stop it--well, except eat.

## 2. Regrets

![Regrets](https://img.allw.mn/content/ta/ad/l1t12xgo_500x236.gif)

Let's face it, we can get a bit emotional on our periods. That's why it's the perfect time for us to think about our lives and every single thing that's wrong with our career and relationships. If you're single or unemployed, now is the time to cry about it. Everyone will understand, so you don't have to be embarrassed about it.

## 3. Naps

![Naps](https://img.allw.mn/content/de/ib/qtphzf90_500x239.gif)

Who wants to get out of their comfy, safe, warm bed when there's no school or work to run off to? If you have the day off, you won't want to leave your room. There's no reason to do so, unless you're heading to the bathroom or to get that donut you've been craving. There's nothing wrong with staying in your PJs all day, so go for it.

## 4. Responsibilities

![Responsibilities](https://img.allw.mn/content/c4/da/e6fvy95d_500x220.gif)

You're in too much pain to focus on the big test you have tomorrow. You know you need to study, but you just can't bring yourself to do it. Instead of being productive, you'll shed a few tears about how you wish you were doing something useful. Once you calm down, you'll realize that the test material isn't all that difficult to memorize.

## 5. Crushes

![Crushes](https://img.allw.mn/content/uv/zl/otly3sid_500x281.gif)

Your hormones go crazy during that time of the month, so you're on the lookout for cute boys. When you see one, it might be difficult to keep your cool. You just want to run over to him and start your happily ever after, but you force yourself to admire him from afar. No sense in embarrassing yourself today.

## 6. Tears

![Tears](https://img.allw.mn/content/t0/jl/zh8xxz48_500x271.gif)

You're feeling bloated, lonely, and just plain miserable. You want to hide away, but you're stuck in the office or in a classroom full of people. You wish you could just crawl under your desk to escape from it all, because you couldn't be in a worse mood.

## 7. Cramps

![Cramps](https://img.allw.mn/content/a7/bi/kan43iyn_500x286.gif)

Emotional pain isn't the only type that we have to deal with. The physical pain can be just as horrible, and for some women, cramps are the worst part of any period. Even if your emotions are stable, it's hard to keep calm when your uterus feels like it's being torn apart.

No one has a fun time on their period. It's not something we look forward to, but it's something we have to deal with, so we have to get used to it. What's your least favorite part about your time of the month?
