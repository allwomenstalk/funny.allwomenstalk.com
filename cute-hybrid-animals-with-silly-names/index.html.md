# 7 Cute Hybrid Animals with Silly Names ...

We've all seen some mighty adorable animals throughout our lives, but there are some cute hybrid animals that you may never have seen. They have some silly names, but that makes them even more precious. If you're an animal lover, than you'll love them all, no matter what they look like. To prove it, here are some cute hybrid animals that you need to see to believe:

## 1. Zebroids

![Zebroids](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qc/g2/w6/fr1ae0r7_600x506.jpg)

Have you ever seen anything like this? This adorable animal is a Zorse, which is a cross between a zebra and a horse. It has the stripes of a zebra, but has white spots that are reminiscent of a gorgeous horse. This is one of the cute hybrid animals that looks pretty wild. It's something rare that you'd be lucky to come across. Of course, there are many types of Zebroids that look different than this one. There are other zebra hybrids called a zonkey, zebonkey, zebronkey, zebrinny, zebrula, zebrass, zedonk, and zebadonk

## 2. Wholphin

![Wholphin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n9/ok/c1/vy33gjz6_600x400.jpg)

If you saw this animal, you might not even realize that it's a hybrid. It looks pretty similar to a dolphin, but it's actually a cross between a male whale and female dolphin. They're extremely rare, because it's a battle to survive after they're born. The first Wholphin that survived in the United States was born in Hawaii and named Kekaimalu, which means 'from the peaceful ocean.'

## 3. Camas

![Camas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t3/wn/go/dtuxbqpl_600x482.jpg)

It's impossible for this camel and llama mix to reproduce without the help of humans because a fully grown camel is six times heavier than a llama. Insemination is the only possible way for this cutie to be born. Only a handful of them have been created. They eat shrubs and other plants, just like camels do.

## 4. Beefalo

![Beefalo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rn/rj/pu/kf9xwm71_600x392.jpg)

This is a cross between domestic cattle and an American bison. They've existed since 1749. They're huge, but they don't look out of the ordinary. You'd probably pass them in a field without a second glance. Of course, that doesn't make them any less impressive. They're still an awesome piece of nature.

## 5. Grolar Bears

![Grolar Bears](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cg/vc/l7/tgywn56m_400x300.jpg)

This grizzly bear and polar bear hybrid is terrifying. Although they're genetically similar, they tend to stay away from each other in the wild, which is why it's quite surprising that they exist at all. As much as we'd like to see them in reality, you wouldn't want to get too close to this breed.

## 6. Cheetoh

![Cheetoh](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q0/ho/aj/gf30sqc3_600x492.jpg)

A Cheetoh is a cross between a bengal cat and an ocicat. It sounds like a delicious orange packed snack, but it's just an adorable animal with a unique neck. It looks rather normal when it's fully grown, but as a kitten, it's as cute as can be! Did you ever see anything this darn cute?

## 7. Toast of Botswana

![Toast of Botswana](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tc/e3/a6/ka4osnej_468x391.jpg)

Some people misidentify these babies as goats, but they're really a combination between a sheep and a goat. By far, it's one of the cutest things out there! The fur is a blend of colors, since it's a hybrid, making it look extra adorable.

These animals may have silly names, but they're just as cute as the animals we're used to seeing. Which one of these hybrids do you think is the most adorable? Are there any other hybrids you love that weren't mentioned, like Ligers?
