# 7 Silly Songs to Listen to While on Your Period ...

If you're feeling sad or grumpy, there are clever songs to listen to while on your period that will put a smile on that beautiful face. You shouldn't focus on the pain you're feeling and let your day be ruined because of it. Instead, you can try to turn your sadness into something worth smiling about. Here are some silly songs to listen to while on your period that should make you feel better:

## 1. The Lazy Song by Bruno Mars

During your time of the month, it's not fun to move around. Relaxing in bed with Netflix sounds better than getting up and doing something productive. This is one of the best songs to listen to while on your period, because the lyrics describe exactly how you feel. You just want to sit around until Mother Nature decides to leave you alone.

## 2. Boys Boys Boys by Lady Gaga

Your hormones can make you more flirtatious than usual. This song works perfectly for those occasions, because it's about how much fun boys can be to look at and hang out with. Sometimes, it's hard to get them off of your mind. There's just so many great things about them, and this song lists them off.

## 3. F*** You by Lily Allen

There's a bit of bad language in this song, so only watch it if you don't mind cursing. It's catchy, so it might get stuck in your head after the first time you hear it. It's sung in an upbeat way, but the lyrics are about how frustrating other people can be.

## 4. Blood by MCR

The chorus of this song describes your period perfectly. It's not pleasant to think about, but we have to deal with it every single month for years to come. The next time you're on your period, listen to this song in order to laugh a little. Hey, it's better than crying.

## 5. Pain by Three Days Grace

The title is self-explanatory. During that time of the month, you're in severe pain. It's not fun to experience, but at least you're feeling something. The pain lets you know that you're alive and well, so try to fight through it. Every woman has to do it.

## 6. If I Were a Boy by Beyonce

If you were a boy, you wouldn't have to deal with the pain of periods. You wouldn't have to waste money on tampons and pads. You wouldn't have to wash blood out of your clothes. Of course, then you wouldn't get to be the fabulous woman you are!

## 7. This is What Makes Us Girls by Lana Del Rey

The most important lyric in this song is, "Don't cry about it." Even though times are rough, you'll make it through. This song helps women feel like they're not alone. No matter what, we'll always have each other. Sure, we have a lot of stressful things to deal with, but we should all be proud of being girls, despite all the troubles we have to deal with!

Your period is painful, stressful, and frustrating. It can cause you to switch between being angry, upset, and overjoyed. Just be happy that, even though it comes every month, it also leaves. What do you do to make yourself feel better when that time of the month comes around?
