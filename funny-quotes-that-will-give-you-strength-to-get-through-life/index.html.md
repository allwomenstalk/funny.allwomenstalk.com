# 7 Funny Quotes That Will Give You Strength to Get through Life ...

When you're having a rough day, funny quotes can cheer you up, especially when they have important meaning behind them. You can't live a fulfilled life in complete sadness, so these quotes will give you the advice you need in order to get out there and be happy again. You deserve to know how wonderful you are and how much you're able to accomplish if you set your mind to it. Here are some funny quotes that prove a point about how you should live your life:

## 1. Sweet Single Life

![Sweet Single Life](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qr/ad/w60lw26u_500x499.jpg)

You don't have to be in a relationship in order to be happy. Sometimes, you're all you need. This is one of the funny quotes that prove that you can have just as much fun being single as you can have in a relationship. Don't assume that you need a partner to be happy. You have yourself, and you're pretty darn awesome!

## 2. Worthy Women

![Worthy Women](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tv/kw/h9obtx94_500x177.jpg)

While you should never smoke or use drugs, this is a valid metaphor. You don't deserve to be treated like you're disposable. You're worthy of love, and shouldn't settle for less. If there are unhealthy people in your life, don't feel guilty about straying from them. You should only hang out with people that make you feel better about yourself, and can make you smile.

## 3. Shopping Spree Smiles

![Shopping Spree Smiles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ez/wk/sr8vbqa5_500x500.jpg)

When you're upset, a trip to the mall can change your attitude. It's amazing how different you can feel after stepping into a new outfit. A change of clothing can help you change your life. So the next time you're feeling down, head to the store to pick out an outfit you can rock.

## 4. Say No to Normality

![Say No to Normality](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mi/hh/mh5567gg_461x395.jpg)

You shouldn't try to fit in by changing your personality. You should be yourself, no matter the cost. You'll never be happy if you're pretending to be someone you're not. Even though it may seem like everybody else acts the same, and you're different, it's not true. Everyone is in the same boat. No two people are exactly alike, because everyone has little quirks of their own.

## 5. Goodbye to Gender

![Goodbye to Gender](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s5/z0/e70vekkb_480x686.jpg)

Who pays attention to traditional gender roles anymore? Men and women are allowed to do whatever they want, despite their sex. There's no reason to let your gender stop you from doing something that you want to do. It's the twenty-first century, and those roles don't apply anymore.

## 6. Dare to Be Different

![Dare to Be Different](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zw/cg/ospytzvp_500x388.jpg)

Just like everyone's body is different, every friendship is different. You can't compare one person to another, because your relationships are all going to be based on different things. Remember that certain friends are people you can trust until you die, and others aren't going to be in your life forever.

## 7. Get up and Go

![Get up and Go](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/go/v7/pyy2ygjf_480x480.jpg)

You won't accomplish anything by sitting around, waiting for good things to come to you. You have to go out and accomplish things on your own. Whether you want a boyfriend or a job, you have to try your best to land one. Nothing is going to happen if you sleep through the day, and refuse to be productive.

These funny quotes might make you chuckle, but they give good life advice. Follow them if you want to be successful--and most importantly, happy. What's the best advice you've ever been given?
