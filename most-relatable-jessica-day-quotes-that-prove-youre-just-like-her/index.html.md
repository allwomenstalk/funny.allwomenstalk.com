# 7 Most Rollicking and Relatable Jessica Day Quotes That Prove Youre Just like Her ...

If you’ve ever watched New Girl, you’ve heard some amazing, hilarious Jessica Day quotes. She’s a quirky girl, but she has qualities that relate to everyone out there. She’s funny, she’s adorable, and she’s so darn lovable. Whether or not you’ve seen the show, here are Jessica Day quotes that you may have said yourself:

## 1. Look, It's Food. I Love Food

![Look, It's Food. I Love Food](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/me/pc/jfo4p2kz_500x231.jpg)

You don't have to be obsessed with salads and celery, just because you're a lady. We love food just as much as men do. This is one of the most relatable Jessica Day quotes, because there's never a bad time to see food. Food can distract people, can lighten the mood, and can help fix awkward moments.

## 2. I Feel like I Want to Murder Someone and Now Also Want Soft Pretzels

![I Feel like I Want to Murder Someone and Now Also Want Soft Pretzels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cv/el/b3tt3c6q_500x266.jpg)

When you're on your period, you have ridiculous cravings that even you don't understand. You want random food, and your mood can switch from happy to angry in a matter of seconds. Jessica puts these feelings into words, even though it makes her sound pretty crazy. Of course, it's what all women go through at one point or another. We've all been there.

## 3. I Just Wanted to Listen to Taylor Swift Alone

![I Just Wanted to Listen to Taylor Swift Alone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ev/j4/tffkyjj9_500x224.jpg)

When you're upset, you don't always want someone to hug you and tell jokes. Sometimes, you just want to listen to your sad music and cry until you can't cry anymore. There's nothing wrong with embracing your sadness, as long as you don't drown in it.

## 4. Ugh Couples

![Ugh Couples](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/om/py/aulewln5_500x230.jpg)

We all realize how great relationships can be when you find the right person to spend your life with. However, when you're single, seeing cute couples can make you sick. You don't want to be reminded that you're alone, so you try to keep your distance. All of that handholding and baby talk--who needs it?

## 5. I Hope You like Feminist Rants, 'cause That's Kind of My Thing

![I Hope You like Feminist Rants, 'cause That's Kind of My Thing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z1/mc/b3t030s4_245x270.jpg)

Some people cringe when they hear the word 'feminist,' because they don't realize what it truly means. If you've ever spoken up about equality, then you should know what Jess is talking about. No one wants to be treated poorly due to their gender, or any other trait. Jess may be adorable, but she also knows how to be strong and independent.

## 6. I like Being Weird

![I like Being Weird](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yv/hl/kpnxq8gk_500x215.jpg)

You might like to make up songs to sing or make bizarre animal noises. You might like dancing around the house naked or saying random words aloud. Everyone enjoys doing weird things, because quite simply, they're fun. Why deprive yourself of entertainment, just because you want to appear normal? You might as well embrace your uniqueness.

## 7. I'm Gonna End up Alone

![I'm Gonna End up Alone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uv/mi/lyhrmz3m_500x281.jpg)

Everyone has a moment or two where they're worried they'll be alone forever. After a breakup or being rejected, you feel like you're just never going to get it right. However, you can't let it get you down! Even amazing girls like Jess, who have everything going for them, worry that they're not good enough to be loved. You'll find happiness one day, so don't get too down.

You might not be exactly like Jessica Day, but you definitely share one or two of her traits. Have you ever watched New Girl? Who's your favorite character on the show?
