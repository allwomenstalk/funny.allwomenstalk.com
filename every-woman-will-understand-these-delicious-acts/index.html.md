# Every Woman Will Understand These 17 Delicious Acts ...

Being a woman is pretty amazing, but there are moments that make it even more special. These are the moments you may not think about very often. They happen every day, so it's easy to take them for granted, but you definitely miss them when you don't experience them. You've enjoyed these acts, right? Even #6?

![Taking off Your Bra at the End of the Day](https://img.allw.mn/content/ym/i0/xo0xjs1b56861168ead0c502325968_500x340.gif)

Is there anything more delicious than this, really? There's just so much freedom.

## 2. Getting Naked Period

![Getting Naked Period](https://img.allw.mn/content/ma/zw/zmjq2w1s5686118b1d8bd706475249_500x281.gif)

No bra, no Spanx, no skinny jeans, no shoes … being naked and unfettered is heaven, especially if you're just running around your house alone, having a high old time.

## 3. Rubbing Your Eye after You Take off Your Mascara

![Rubbing Your Eye after You Take off Your Mascara](https://img.allw.mn/content/ul/hr/t87l6lsm568612435b21e356035462_500x281.gif)

You know that sad little eye-scratch you do with the side of your fingertip when your eye makeup is all on point? Those things suck. There's nothing like finally being able to rub the hell out of your eyes.

## 4. A Smoothly Shaven Leg

![A Smoothly Shaven Leg](https://img.allw.mn/content/jy/mi/azoba03k5686127e8510f214602301_480x270.gif)

I hate shaving. Hate it. But as much as I hate it, even I have to admit that my legs feel awesome afterward. I just want to touch them constantly.

## 5. Taking out Bobby Pins

![Taking out Bobby Pins](https://img.allw.mn/content/ws/ps/ci0v20sr568612aa7b1d2561216343_500x281.gif)

Especially if any of them have been poking you in the head or pulling your hair the wrong way. Absolute bliss!

## 6. Farting in Private

![Farting in Private](https://img.allw.mn/content/de/xb/cm0bc2mw568612cd56a6d177802767_500x282.gif)

What? It's true. Holding in a fart feels awful. The freedom of finally blowing the old butt trumpet is right up there with taking off your bra.

## 7. Removing All Shapewear

![Removing All Shapewear](https://img.allw.mn/content/gd/ip/cs4midub568612f0ef1a6398182543_500x280.gif)

This goes beyond both getting naked and removing your bra. Sliding out of your Spanx, pulling off a body shaper, and undoing a corset are all amazing. They're practically orgasmic.

## 8. Peeing after a Long Day

![Peeing after a Long Day](https://img.allw.mn/content/pj/a0/mm3innvw568612fe5c686529618598_500x281.gif)

## 9. Taking off Your High Heels

![Taking off Your High Heels](https://img.allw.mn/content/jm/ow/xt94xuwq5686132c4c11e873555881_500x250.gif)

These medieval torture devices might make your legs look out-of-this-world, but taking them off at the end of the day is heavenly.

## 10. Popping a Zit

![Popping a Zit](https://img.allw.mn/content/z6/il/uobt5vvu56861341b1c51211563715_500x375.gif)

You know you shouldn't, but you can't help it.

## 11. Plucking a Pesky Hair

![Plucking a Pesky Hair](https://img.allw.mn/content/e1/h7/eo14600d5686135e6a094533496817_500x278.gif)

Whether it's on your chin or your eyebrows, you know the joy of successfully plucking a stubborn hair.

## 12. Taking Your Hair down

![Taking Your Hair down](https://img.allw.mn/content/kz/ph/r3q7qmrk5686137db63e0799436089_500x260.gif)

Pulling out a ponytail, unfurling a braid, releasing a bun – bliss, heaven, so sweet.

## 13. Getting Your Eyebrows Done

![Getting Your Eyebrows Done](https://img.allw.mn/content/vt/e1/ucti0n2w568613a885a40101629936_500x250.gif)
