# 7 Funny Quotes for Single Women ...

It can be fun to be in a relationship, but it's just as exciting being on the market, which is why there are so many funny quotes for single women. Don't waste your time moping around when you could be enjoying your single status. There are so many places to go and people to see when you're not stuck hanging out with the same boy week after week. Whether you're looking for a boyfriend or are happy with your freedom, you can appreciate these funny quotes for single women:

## 1. Distant Dudes

![Distant Dudes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c3/z0/warjtvx0_420x294.jpg)

If you're sick and tired of others asking when you're going to get a boyfriend, you can borrow this quote the next time you're questioned about it. Does it really matter whether or not you're currently in a relationship? All that matters is that you're happy with your life. Some people just don't understand that, which is why this is one of the funny quotes for single women to enjoy.

## 2. Made up Men

![Made up Men](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/x9/e6xr75n0_600x312.jpg)

How could you be happy with a guy in your real life when there are so many amazing fictional characters and celebrities for you to admire from afar? You may never get to kiss them, but you can daydream about it all you want. Plus, they'll never get the chance to hurt you. They're the perfect partner.

## 3. Sleeping Soundly

![Sleeping Soundly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n5/c3/jle91bia_600x375.jpg)

Cuddling can be nice and cozy, but you should enjoy having your bed to yourself while it lasts. When you're alone, you don't have to worry about the blankets getting torn off of you or hitting someone in your sleep. You're completely free to move around and find the best position to fall asleep in.

## 4. Fans of Food

![Fans of Food](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hd/dz/rdhjh70e_550x533.jpg)

Here's a new saying that you can start to spread. If you had to choose between fries and guys, you'd just have to pick the food. You have no other choice, because there's no point in living without your favorite snack. Yeah, relationships are nice, but your lips would rather taste a nice hot fry than a nice [hot guy](https://love.allwomenstalk.com/hot-guys-which-one-is-your-type/), don't you think?

## 5. Owned and Operated

![Owned and Operated](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c9/yc/dzrokb11_420x294.jpg)

For some reason, being "single" has a lot of negative connotations. There should be a new name for it that lets others know that you're an independent woman who is happy with her life. Just because you're single, it doesn't mean that you're looking. Relationships aren't for everyone.

## 6. For the Freedom

![For the Freedom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z0/x7/vym0obbg_600x238.jpg)

The best part about being single is that you can do what you want, when you want. If your phone dies, you don't have to worry about checking in with your partner. If you want to flirt with a hot guy at a party, you don't have to worry about being unfaithful. You're allowed to behave in whatever way you wish.

## 7. Switch and save

![Switch and save](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h2/on/pt2omz7n_420x294.jpg)

Think of all the money you save by not being in a relationship. You don't have to buy a holiday gift, or a present for their birthday, or for Valentine's day, or for your anniversary. Without a man, you'll be rolling in the dough. You could take a trip or buy a new car with all of your extra cash!

If you're single, you shouldn't fight to change your status. When it happens, it happens. There's no sense in rushing it, because you can be just as fulfilled without a boyfriend as you would be with a boyfriend. Are you enjoying the single life?
