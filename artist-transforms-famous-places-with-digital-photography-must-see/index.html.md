# Artist Transforms Famous Places with Digital Photography \-MUST SEE !

After aspiring violinist Julia Borzucka was injured, she turned her talents to sketching, and her work is so amazing, transforming familiar photos into backgrounds for her characters. Has she worked her magic on a place you love? Let's find out.

## 1. London

![London Eye, reflection, ferris wheel, morning, evening,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bn/gn/h5ru0ln557f3caf4bc8c3222566200_600x400.jpg)

This giant hamster has no interest in wreaking Gozdilla-like havoc on his city. No, he just wants to exercise.

## 2. Table for One?

![skyscraper, metropolis, tower block, city, landmark,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ne/to/z70v0p5057f3caf520964111929050_600x600.jpg)

## 3. New York City

![New York City, Empire State Building, metropolitan area, skyline, skyscraper,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zf/of/s076166a57f3caf54901d611678010_600x400.jpg)

## 4. San Francisco

![sea, sailing, vacation, jumping, windsports,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/em/n5/v4ry1pya57f3caf57845b518019876_600x400.jpg)

## 5. The Lake

![aerial photography, landmark, atmosphere of earth, flight, extreme sport,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lj/v1/gg14f9cm57f3caf5d0f37379653494_600x600.jpg)

## 6. Earth
