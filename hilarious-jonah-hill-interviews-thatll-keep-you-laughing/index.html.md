# 7 Hilarious Jonah Hill Interviews Thatll Keep You Laughing ...

There are so many incredibly funny Jonah Hill interviews that'll make your stomach hurt from so much laughing! I may or may not have binge-watched all of his interviews on YouTube and have come to the conclusion that Jonah's my new favorite person. He's just so humble and genuine! My favorite thing about him is how effortless it is for him to make people laugh. You can tell it brings him so much joy! Check out some of my favorite Jonah Hill interviews.

## 1. Get Him to the Greek: Myspace Live Australia

I've seen this interview way too many times and somehow it gets funnier EACH time. How is that possible?! The video that Jonah mentions in the interview is HILARIOUS by the way - I definitely suggest looking it up if you haven't already. I love how Jonah is so genuine with his answers, he seems like he's so easy to get along with! This video is sure to bring you tons of laughs. It's just one of many Jonah Hill interviews that are so funny!

## 2. 21 Jump St

With over a million views, this interview went viral! Jonah and Channing have the cutest bromance and all their interviews together are SO funny. Jonah was even being his charming self and complimenting the interview - LOL! I honestly don't know how the interviewer held her composure when the two were cracking up. I would've joined in with the laughing!

## 3. 22 Jump St: Unscripted Moviefone Interview

I don't know about you, but I can spend hours watching unscripted interviews. I especially love the ones of Jonah Hill and Channing Tatum, of course! In this interview, Jonah reveals what he'd do if woke up one day and discovered he was Channing Tatum. The two then discuss Bieber-related Twitter names, who they'd cast in a live-action remake of The Little Mermaid, and more! Their laughs are so contagious.

## 4. The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon

In this interview, Jonah talks about his '22 Jump St' co-star, Ice Cube. I love the fact that Jonah is a huge fan of his and isn't afraid of letting us all know just how much he loves him! The story he shares is so funny, I can just picture the whole thing!

## 5. KISS FM (Uk) Radio Interview

I know I've included a lot of interviews of Jonah WITH Channing Tatum, but can you blame me?! The two are such a funny duo! In this interview, Jonah talks about the set life in Puerto Rico, the secret to making a comedy, and more!

## 6. Kidd Kraddick in the Morning

In this super adorable video, Jonah explains the meaning behind his tattoos! One of which includes a tattoo of a heart with the words "nanny rules" inside. How sweet is that?! He also shared a story about his ex girlfriend, which was pretty funny!

## 7. Throwback Interview

This interview is a MAJOR throwback, back to when Jonah was promoting 'Superbad'! It's a hilarious interview where Jonah freaks out at the interviewer's questions and walks away. What so many failed to realize was that it was all staged! It's a skit and even featured on the Superbad DVD. It'll still make you laugh though!

Well, there you have it. These are just seven out of MANY awesomely hilarious Jonah Hill interviews! Doesn't he just make you wish you guys were best friends? Of the list, which interview did you like best?
