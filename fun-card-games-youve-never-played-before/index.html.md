# 7 Fun Card Games You've Never Played before ...

You don't need to buy expensive game systems and exercise equipment in order to have fun with your friends. All you really need is a simple pack of cards. Once you're bored of playing War and Go Fish, you can move on to playing games none of you have ever tried before. It shouldn't take you all that long to understand the rules to the following games, so get ready to play!

## 1. Buffalo

![Buffalo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nh/xx/mlf6hsvf55a21de192099586383486_600x402.jpg)

$21 on [tiltfactor.org](https://goo.gl/nRu3ry)

When you play this game, you're going to separate cards into two stacks. During your turn, you need to flip over two cards and come up with a person who fits into both categories. If one card says "British" and the other says "Wizard," then you can use Harry Potter as your answer.

## 2. Deck around

_ $36 on deckaround.com_

This is a game that Cards Against Humanity fans will love. It contains decks of cards with different slang words on it that you may or may not have heard of. Once a card is selected, everyone has to write down a made-up definition of the word. Then everyone's made-up definitions will be read aloud with the real definition. If you trick someone into believing your definition was the real one, you get points.

## 3. Personally Incorrect

![Personally Incorrect](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xk/m1/ohfuejn6559fbae036728522976410_600x759.jpg)

$22 on [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is another game that is pretty similar to Cards Against Humanity, because it can be highly offensive. Except, during this game, you're going to be insulting your own friends. You'll receive a card that says something like "When INSERT NAME was a child, he used to draw BLANK." Then you need to use the name of someone in the room and ask players to fill in the blank with a card that they believe fits the best.

## 4. Dirty Minds

![Dirty Minds](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n2/an/p3oyi6qi559fbadf89799276840641_500x441.jpg)

$13 on [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This game is R-rated, so make sure you're playing with people who aren't afraid to be naughty. All you have to do is pick up a card and read the description, which will give you clues to a certain word. Even though the correct word is clean, the clues are meant to make dirty words pop into your mind at first, making it hard for you to come up with the correct answer.

## 5. Game of Phones

![Game of Phones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wn/a5/nuvo8m26559fbae07ba67365240503_600x274.jpg)

$20 on [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a card game that requires you to use your phone. It'll ask you to do things like show your friends the last photograph you took or to race to see who will be the first person to get a text back from their parents. This is the game of the future!

## 6. Loaded Questions

![Loaded Questions](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xb/cg/k3mmv3f7559fbae046ac8893489131_600x634.jpg)

$34 on [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a card game that requires you to do a bit of writing. You'll pick up a card that contains a question like, "What's your favorite book?" Everyone else at the table will write down their answers and then someone will read them all aloud to you. Once they do, you have to try to decide which person said what. It'll help you see how well you know all your friends.

## 7. Rotten Apples

![Rotten Apples](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kf/t4/d5rq3eo3559fbae138415645760664_600x484.jpg)

$20 on [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

This is a combination of Apples to Apples and Cards Against Humanity. If you love both of those games, then you need to pick up this one. It's a little raunchy, but all of the best games are!

These games can provide you with endless hours of entertainment. What's your favorite card game?
