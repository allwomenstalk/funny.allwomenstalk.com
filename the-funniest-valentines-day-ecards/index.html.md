# The Funniest Valentines Day ECards ...

Valentine's Day isn't as bad as everyone says it is. Even if you hate the holiday, you can make it feel a little more fun by poking some fun at it. In order to cheer you up, here are the funniest Valentine's day ecards that you should send your friends on February 14:

## 1. Forever Alone

![Forever Alone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fw/z7/zg4q0rgi56a0f32fa4e76979231657_425x237.jpg)

There's no reason to cry on Valentine's Day, just because you're single. After all, you've been single for a while, and you didn't feel the need to cry over it every single day. Valentine's Day isn't any different than any other day of the year, so why would you waste your time getting upset over it?

## 2. Social Media

![Social Media](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ib/o6/cpcrdayj56a0f33027525335781975_425x237.jpg)

If you log onto social media to complain about how much you hate Valentine's Day, then everyone is going to assume that you're bitter over being single. You don't want that to happen, which is why you should probably keep your thoughts to yourself. Let your couple friends have a nice holiday without trying to suck the fun out of it for them.

## 3. Snowy Streets

![Snowy Streets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/da/nr/b8jgmkzl56a0f33016731774965850_425x237.jpg)

Even though we didn't have a white Christmas, we might end up having a white Valentine's Day. If that's the case, then the streets are going to get plowed. Of course, it won't be the only thing getting plowed. Plenty of couples will choose to celebrate in the best way they know how. Why do you think that there are so many summer babies born each year?

## 4. Secret Admirer

![Secret Admirer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sq/ij/z80r2s7t56a0f330858fc735081415_600x334.jpg)

You don't need to receive a card on Valentine's Day in order to feel special. Of course, if it would make you feel better, there's nothing wrong with sending yourself a card or a bouquet of flowers. No one else will know that you're the sender, so at least it'll make you look more popular than you feel. Of course, why buy flowers when you could buy yourself a brand new outfit and some jewels?

## 5. Cupid's Arrow

![Cupid's Arrow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dp/n8/ltgsuj2q56a0f330a0f8f779002928_425x237.jpg)

When you take a second to think about Cupid, it might freak you out a little bit. After all, a naked baby flying through the air and tossing arrows at strangers is a little strange. That's why you don't want to think too much into it. Just go with the flow and enjoy the holiday without thinking about the concepts behind it.

## 6. On a Diet

![On a Diet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/lk/s2c4dyas56a0f33099e1b572973741_420x294.jpg)

If you're still trying to keep your New Year's Resolution of losing weight, then you probably don't want to end up binging on chocolate. That's why you should tell your boyfriend that you won't be accepting candy this year. If he wants to buy you a gift, then he can go to Jared and buy you some diamonds. It's the least he can do if he wants to support you on your diet.

## 7. Celebrating Valentine's Day

![Celebrating Valentine's Day](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/id/to9unp5356a0f330cef84512815506_425x237.jpg)

Don't assume that all of your couple friends are having more fun than you are. Some of them will end up having a horrible holiday at an overly packed restaurant. So instead of sulking about being single, you should be happy that you're getting to save your money and your sanity.

Try to enjoy the holiday as much as you can! Are you planning on doing anything special this Valentine's Day?
