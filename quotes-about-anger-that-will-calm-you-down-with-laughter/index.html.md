# 7 Quotes about Anger That Will Calm You down with Laughter ...

**Anger management** can sometimes feel like trying to control a herd of stampeding elephants with just a toothpick. It’s not impossible, but it’s certainly a challenge, especially with the world as wild as it is in 2024. Seriously, who hasn’t found themselves furiously muttering under their breath over a tech glitch or a particularly vexing email? We’ve all been there! But what if I told you there are seven _hilarious quotes_ about anger that might just hit your funny bone while soothing your fury?

Back when I was in university, my psych professor always said that _laughter is the best medicine_. Sure, it’s a cliché, but there's a nugget of truth buried under that old adage. When you’re miffed about something-whether it's due to an argument with a friend or the latest season of your favorite show getting postponed-sometimes the best remedy is a hearty chuckle. Yep, even a _funny quote_ can flip your perspective. And honestly, who doesn’t want a quick and easy way to cool down?

Now, I'm not saying laughter is going to solve all your problems. That would be, well, laughable (pun intended). But integrating humor into your coping mechanisms can be a game-changer. In fact, check out our [subsection on “The Health Benefits of Humor”](#3). You'll be surprised how intertwined chuckles and well-being are. If anger were a fire, consider laughter the delightful splash of water to help it simmer down.

I remember once, I had a spat with my roommate over the classic debate of who ate the last piece of pizza. We both ended up sulking for hours. Then, I stumbled upon a quote that said, "Holding onto anger is like drinking poison and expecting the other person to die”-classic, right? We burst out laughing. The pizza drama deflated and we shared the last, not-so-fresh bite together (oh, uni life!).

The key takeaway here is: finding light even in dark moments can transform your whole experience. Anger, when left unchecked, can sabotage your happiness, but a touch of humor, an unexpected _funny quote_, and voila! You've got a new lease on life.

This might sound too idealistic, but trust me, it works. Whether it’s a minor irritation or a full-blown rage-fest, having these _hilarious quotes_ in your arsenal is like having a secret weapon. So dive into this guide with an open heart, and let’s tackle those _anger issues_ with a hearty belly laugh. Explore more in our [section on “Why Laughter Works”](#7). After all, why stay angry when you can laugh it away?

## 1. Bone Breaker

![Bone Breaker](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bm/s1/w5jtxfwy_494x393.jpg)

We all know violence isn't the answer, but it won't stop us at laughing at this quote about anger. I mean, wouldn't you rather break a leg and wear a cast for a few months than get dumped and wonder what you did wrong for the rest of eternity? At least there's a way to bandage up your limbs. Your heart is a little less cooperative when you try to fix it up.

## 2. Anger Issues

![Anger Issues](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i8/n1/frq65wao_600x403.jpg)

If you get angry easily, you cant blame yourself, because it might not be your fault. You might just be surrounded by incredibly irritating people. If you relocate and end up with a new group, those "anger issues" everyone claims you have may vanish. It's all about who you choose to associate with, so stay away from the bad seeds.

## 3. Minor Misunderstandings

![Minor Misunderstandings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q5/lq/gnrqdqx1_500x359.jpg)

Sometimes, you'll start a fight without realizing that you're doing so. You'll think that you're explaining yourself in a calm manner, but everyone else will shrink away from you out of fright. To avoid a misunderstanding, you need to watch your tone and choice of words. You don't need those pointless fights in your life.

## 4. Feeling Fine

![Feeling Fine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oz/bq/bnhdfcm9_525x278.jpg)

Men complain about women being overly emotional, so when they ask us what's wrong, there's no wonder why we lie to them. We've all said that we're fine when we're not or that nothing is wrong when it is, which is why men are even smart enough to know that the words are a bad sign.

## 5. Comfort Candy

![Comfort Candy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/te/nk/secrb920_540x363.jpg)

Who doesn't love affection? When you're angry, having your boyfriend wrap you in his arms can be all you need to calm down. However, if he's the one you're mad at, that gesture could be dangerous. That's why comfort food can be the better option for any gender.

## 6. Rest and Relaxation

![Rest and Relaxation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l6/ji/pc8n67gp_420x294.jpg)

Even if you know you need therapy, you might not be able to afford it. If that's the case, there are plenty of things that can help you relax instead. Drink some coffee, pet a puppy, or take kickboxing lessons. Whatever works for you is fine.

## 7. Okay to Cry

![Okay to Cry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pc/we/b1hoy68f_420x294.jpg)

Some people cry whenever they get angry. It's a frustrating thing, because then others won't take you seriously. However, there's nothing wrong with shedding a few tears. It's a normal reaction when you're overwhelmed with emotion. It doesn't make you any weaker than your opponent.

Anger is an emotion you'll feel for as long as your heart's still beating, which is why you need to learn to deal with it in a healthy way. You don't want it to destroy you, so be careful. How do you calm yourself down when you get angry?
