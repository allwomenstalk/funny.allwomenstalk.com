# 7 Silly but Inspirational Robin Williams Genie Quotes from Aladdin ...

Growing up you probably loved hearing Robin Williams' Genie quotes. He was an incredible actor, whether he was playing an inspirational English teacher or a magical, animated creature. Genie is the funniest character in Aladdin, but he still had some touching moments that taught us how to be a good friend. Here are some of the best Robin Williams' Genie quotes that you might remember:

## 1. Tell Her the Truth

"If you wanna court the little lady, you gotta be a straight shooter. Do you got it?" Genie has been around for decades, which is why he knows a bit about love. Even though he doesn't look like a ladies' man, the advice he gave Aladdin about wooing Jasmine is accurate. If you want to date someone, you can't throw lies at them. You have to tell them the truth, so that they get to know the real you. This is one of Robin Williams' Genie quotes that you should take into consideration the next time you're in a room with your crush.

## 2. Make over

"First, that fez and vest combo is much too third century. These patches. What are we trying to say? Beggar? No. Let's work with me here." It's important to have a professional appearance. if you wish to be successful. Listen to Genie, and try to dress your best when you're hoping to land a new job or impress your crush.

## 3. Enough about You. Talk about Her

"She's smart, fun. The hair, the eyes. Anything. Pick a feature." Compliments go a long way, and you know it. Instead of always bragging about your own accomplishments, praise your crush for their triumphs. There must be something impressive about them if you like them, so it shouldn't be all that hard to shower them with compliments.

## 4. Oh, to Be Free

"To be my own master. Such a thing would be greater than all the magic and all the treasures in all the world." Never take your freedom for granted. Sure, your parents might make you come home at midnight, but you still have your freedom of speech. Don't forget how special that right is.

## 5. Excuse Me?

"Did you bring me here? And all of a sudden you're walking out on me? I don't think so, not right now." Be like Genie and don't let others take advantage of you. When Aladdin treated Genie with disrespect, he called him out on it, because he knew he deserved better. So do you.

## 6. Through Richer or Poorer

"No matter what anyone else says, you'll always be a prince to me." Genie doesn't care whether Aladdin is rich or poor, because he's a good friend. When you find someone special, never let them go, and never let them think they're not valuable. Constantly remind them of how magical they are.

## 7. You're Unique

"You ain't never had a friend like me." There's no one in the world like you. You may not be as unique as a blue, floating Genie, but you're still unique. Be proud of your differences, because they're what make you you.

Even though Robin Williams is gone, we won't be able to forget him. He left behind dozens of amazing films that we can go back and watch whenever we want to hear his cheery voice again. What's your favorite Robin Williams' movie?
