# The 11 Funniest Commercials from Superbowl 50 ...

You don't have to be a football fan to love the Superbowl: there's the half-time shows, the silly commentary, and of course, the commercials! Superbowl 50 had some weird ones, for sure (puppy-monkey-baby was just wrong), but there were some funny ones, too. Here's a rundown of the funniest (and best) commercials from Superbowl 50.

## 1. Toyota Prius "Longest Chase"

This commercial gets all the wins. It's so funny, right, a Prius as a getaway car, fast, nimble, and quiet? Apparently, the new Prius is all of those things, and more.

## 2. Toyota Prius "Hunters"

"They'll never see us coming." ... "Except for the lights on top, sir." Ha ha! This follow-up to the first commercial is just as clever.

## 3. T-Mobile "Drop the Balls"

This one's funny, too, a spin on Steve Harvey's mega-blunder at the Miss Universe pageant. He's a pretty good sport about it!

## 4. Heinz "Wiener Stampede"

I loved this commercial, but it would have been so much better with an upbeat song, amirite?

## 5. Doritos "Ultrasound"

Myself, I didn't think this ad was very funny (I'm just tired of the "stupid guy" trope), but so many of my friends did, I had to add it to my list.

## 6. Superbowl 50 NFL Ad

This is funny, but who really wants to think about their parents' post-Superbowl celebratory smushing? Anyway, it's clever and I liked the idea.

## 7. SNICKERS “Marilyn”

Every year, Snickers raises the bar for what a truly funny Superbowl commercial should be, and now I already can't wait to see next year's spot.

## 8. Taco Bell "Bigger than"

The full version of this ad is just the best, worth it for George Takei at the end, the Aliens guy, and man buns. Pop culture overload, but funny!

## 9. Amazon Echo Ad

Really the only big-time funny of this ad is the Superbowl vs. Oscar bit between Dan Marino and Alec Baldwin. Of course it's nice to see Missy Elliot and Jason Schwartzman again, too...

## 10. Kia "Walken Closet"

I will never, ever wear beige socks again, I promise.

## 11. Hyundai Ryan Reynolds Ad

My favorite part: IN MY FACE! Ha! I love it! Now why can't we have Ryan Reynolds in ALL of our commercials?

Which ad from Superbowl 50 did you like best?
