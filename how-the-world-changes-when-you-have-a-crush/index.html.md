# How the World Changes when You Have a Crush ...

[College Humor](https://bit.ly/1njcbIh) knows what it's like to have a crush. In fact, Allison Strejlau has even illustrated it.

## 1. Texting

![Texting](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/xd/nfmvkp9c56688ec9c95e4070355113_600x372.jpg)

Whenever your crush texts you, it'll feel like a dream come true, even if he's just saying something casual to you.

## 2. Jealousy

![Jealousy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z3/ig/n0sykz5n56688eca2acd5375158883_600x372.jpg)

If he looks at another girl, you're going to get angry. And if he talks to that girl? You'll be enraged.

## 3. Compliments

![Compliments](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zh/ip/yeomiggu56688eca519a2179783719_600x372.jpg)

They could give you the same compliment that your mom gives you, but you'd value his so much more.

## 4. Waiting

![Waiting](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/vn/in14myyk56688eca75be3666750517_600x372.jpg)

Two days without them feels like two years.

## 5. Dates

![Dates](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pq/gq/nsy05f4056688ecac0cc5100169250_600x372.jpg)

Dates are supposed to be fun, but it's hard not to stress out over them when you really like the other person.

Do you think this is a pretty accurate description of the way you behave when you have a crush?
