# 7 Funny Quotes for Foodies Who Love to Cook ...

If you're obsessed with tasting the finest meals at the fanciest restaurants, or spending your nights cooking until the sun rises, you'll love the following quotes for foodies. Everybody enjoys eating, so if you're a chef in the making, you must be one likable gal. People will flock to your house once they see your kitchen light turn on, begging for snacks. If you can't stop thinking about the next meal you're going to cook or eat, you'll get a kick out of these quotes for foodies:

## 1. We All Need Wine

![We All Need Wine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d1/fp/e5f954zo_600x408.jpg)

Every great cook knows that wine can be a useful ingredient. But sometimes, it's hard to pour it onto our meals without stealing a sip or two. Those few sips can turn into a few glasses, and then a few bottles, and then there's no wine left to cook with! This is one of the funniest quotes for foodies, because wine is such an important ingredient.

## 2. Silly Standards

![Silly Standards](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ix/rp/ba061jiz_600x448.jpg)

If someone doesn't like your cooking, kick them out of the kitchen. When you're the one holding the knives, you're in charge. If they don't like it, then they can either cook the next meal on their own or they can lower their standards. Most people are lazy, so they'll choose the latter.

## 3. Cheer the Chef

![Cheer the Chef](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/xd/n3x5yzye_570x342.jpg)

Saying that you love to cook is quite different from saying you're a good cook. You may have the time of your life in the kitchen, but have no clue what you're doing. If you're just winging it, then the smoke alarm might try to warn you about your carelessness. If you hear it go off, you might need some assistance the next time you step in front of a stove.

## 4. Enjoy Eating

![Enjoy Eating](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ex/ye/odkxe928_600x450.jpg)

Julia Child is one of the most famous chefs out there, and she didn't realize how talented she was until she was older. If you've never cooked before, you might as well try it to see if you're the next big thing. If you're not, you can always go back to eating. Nothing wrong with that.

## 5. Skeptical of the Skinny

![Skeptical of the Skinny](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gs/iz/ki6haoft_600x403.jpg)

If you're a cook, you have to taste your food. When you're cooking something delicious, it's impossible to stop eating after one bite. You just want to keep going, because food is one of the world's greatest gifts.

## 6. Food Comes First

![Food Comes First](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kj/ik/m3w831zk_489x341.jpg)

Eating keeps us alive. We need food to survive and it keeps us happy, so why would you want to do anything else? Eat first, and then you can finish doing whatever you need to get done. Just make sure that you get that hunger to disappear. When it comes back in a few hours, repeat.

## 7. Helpful Husbands

![Helpful Husbands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ob/yg/et3y6t4r_420x294.jpg)

Some of us aren't willing to cook, which is why it's nice to find a man who loves to mix spices together. When you feel like eating a meal that isn't microwavable, you can go to him for help. You won't have the satisfaction of completing something yourself, but you'll be proud of what a great man you found.

Cooking takes talent, but even if you're bad at it, you can still have fun by messing around in the kitchen. As long as you don't set fire to the house, you can pat yourself on the back. What's your favorite meal to cook?
