# 7 CotDammitElizabeth Videos to Get You into Working out Groove ...

If you checked Facebook in the last couple of weeks, you’ve probably seen several CotDammitElizabeth videos pop up on your newsfeed. The couple known for the comedic snapchat video series is blowing up on the internet and for a good reason too. Their videos are light-hearted, funny and maybe a little bit too relatable for every girl who has been on a diet before. Check out the videos below for a little bit of a pick me up and inspiration!

## 1. “when Ur Girlfriend Asks U to Help Her with Her Diet”

We all know that the hardest part of getting fit is not even motivating yourself to exercise but avoiding unhealthy delicious foods we are so used to consuming. You probably heard the saying “Abs are made in the kitchen” which refers to the fact that your diet has a much greater impact on your fitness than any form of exercise. So remember to avoid junk food or you’ll be “one nugget away from getting 0 likes on your next Instagram”!

## 2. “when Ur Girlfriend Steals Your Snacks”

When it comes to weight loss, the people you surround yourself with will have a major impact on your progress. It’s hard to resist a few cupcakes when your friends decide to indulge in a midnight snack or pass on other dessert when you’re hanging out with your family. Just take a look at Elizabeth and Nancy!

## 3. “when Ur Girlfriend Wants You to Help Her Get That Summer Body”

Free fitness coach and a boyfriend all in one? Count me in! Let’s be honest ladies sometimes we go way too easy on ourselves and either give up or don’t put in 100% of our effort. Maybe if we had someone to push us harder or if we mentally hustled through the pain ourselves, we’d see faster results?

## 4. “when Ur Girlfriend Asks You to Wake Her up for Work!”

Who doesn’t feel like Elizabeth every once in a while? When all you want to do is sleep and eat, and never get out of the house? Personally I know the feeling all too well. Maybe this is why the videos are doing so well, we all see a little bit of ourselves in Elizabeth!

## 5. “when Ur Girlfriend is Obsessed with Food”

Obsessed with food? So are we, but you must remain strong. Stick to fruits, veggies, whole grains for the majority of the week and allow yourself a few cheat meals. Just remember not to overdo, the key is moderation! After all hard work should be rewarded but not to set back your progress.

## 6. “when Ur Girlfriend Goes through Withdrawals”

And when your withdrawals get too strong like they did for Elizabeth in this video, take it as a sign that you are not balancing your diet correctly. Avoid binging later on by indulging just a bit here and there with healthier alternatives. This way you’ll never get caught red handed!

## 7. “when U Buy Ur Girlfriend Junk Good on Her Cheat Day!!”

This video portrays the unfortunate typical crankiness that comes with diets, it’s ugly and unavoidable! Once again keep your hangry self hidden from others, by listening to your cravings and working with better alternatives to damaging foods to your health and body.

Which clip will you be replaying in your head to get into a workout mood?
