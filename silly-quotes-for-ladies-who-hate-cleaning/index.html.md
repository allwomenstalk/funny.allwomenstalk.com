# 11 Silly Quotes for Ladies Who Hate Cleaning ...

Do you put off cleaning for as long as you can? If you do, then [Good Housekeeping](https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/home/cleaning/g2957/funny-cleaning-quotes/) has some relatable quotes that you'll find hilarious. Here they are:

## 1. Excuse the Mess

![Excuse the Mess](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bw/rb/uyv5gkyz564dda1d9a852783694091_480x480.jpg)

It's impossible to keep a house completely clean when there are actual people living in it.

## 2. Save the Earth

![Save the Earth](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ha/jg/rsrl8045564dda1d67425957941013_480x480.jpg)

The dishes aren't exciting enough to make us want to do them.

## 3. On the Sofa

![On the Sofa](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uk/f3/ot07e2qp564dda1dd99a1744958744_480x480.jpg)

No one wants to actually move the couch to get all the gunk that's underneath.

## 4. Getting Something Dirty

![Getting Something Dirty](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ee/ar/pqopokog564dda1e1f16e037809990_480x480.jpg)

Dirt is part of the natural order of things. You can't escape it.

## 5. Clothes Basket

![Clothes Basket](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/c8/wdf7zag3564dda1df40dc182316622_480x480.jpg)

This is pretty gross, but also pretty accurate.

## 6. Hate Housework

![Hate Housework](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ek/u7/ji829hxs564dda1e7c693147441587_480x480.jpg)

As soon as you're done cleaning, something else needs to be cleaned.

## 7. Why Take a Chance?

![Why Take a Chance?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hc/yx/p1i8oglr564dda1ea0659548411884_480x480.jpg)

It's better to be safe than to be sorry.

## 8. Dust is Protective

![Dust is Protective](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/nv/nimeuu0t564dda1e9ce10497665567_480x480.jpg)

That's why you don't want to remove it.

## 9. Superwoman

![Superwoman](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h1/zq/pngtvsmw564dda1f0369a915569796_480x480.jpg)

Now that's a talented woman!

## 10. Cleaning with Kids

![Cleaning with Kids](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/da/kb0fgmte564dda1f245f6180527883_480x480.jpg)

Why clean something that's only going to get messy again?

## 11. Theory on Housework

![Theory on Housework](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rz/bp/y4t1ikwr564dda1fa04f3172527119_480x480.jpg)
