# Never Say These 7 Things to a Girl with Piercings ...

You love your piercings. Unfortunately, the rest of the world doesn't feel the same. That's why [Seventeen](https://www.seventeen.com/fashion/a35892/things-never-say-girl-with-piercings/) has created a list of things that women with piercings are tired of hearing.

## 1. I Would Never Date a Girl with Piercings

![I Would Never Date a Girl with Piercings](https://img.allw.mn/content/fw/ug/nqvrvhbq566593190a312760722001_500x331.gif)

## 2. Did That Hurt?

![Did That Hurt?](https://img.allw.mn/content/ag/bc/r3mscpzh5665931925b6c940146857_450x298.gif)

This question is asked time and time again. You're sick of answering it by now.

## 3. Can You Eat with That in?

![Can You Eat with That in?](https://img.allw.mn/content/vq/ue/xbuzju6u56659319ba9a1237511888_500x345.gif)

Obviously you're not giving up the ability to eat, just to look fashionable.

## 4. Why Did You do That to Yourself?

![Why Did You do That to Yourself?](https://img.allw.mn/content/je/w6/toqidae25665931aa34d6700737275_491x306.gif)

It's because you think it looks hot. And it does.

## 5. You'll Never Get a Job

![You'll Never Get a Job](https://img.allw.mn/content/ir/uh/irxdx0ql5665931a05e50338509447_492x260.gif)
