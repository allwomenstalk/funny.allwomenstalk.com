# 7 Most Hilarious Sketches on YouTube ...

We've all watched hilarious sketches on YouTube; there must be millions of clips that make us laugh. But laughter is a good thing, and you can never have enough of it! So I've picked out my favorite hilarious sketches on YouTube to make you giggle …

## 1. ¿Que Hora Es?

I've stumbled across many hilarious sketches on YouTube, but none make me laugh as much as this one. Every time I watch it, I cry with laughter. If you understand basic Spanish you'll love it too. Imagine a Mexican soap opera (they're very over the top) performed by actors who only know simple phrases in Spanish …

## 2. Grammar Nazis

I love good English, so it doesn't bother me to be called a Grammar Nazi. And here we have that idea taken to extremes, in which a German officer interrogates a Frenchman he suspects of hiding fugitives. But this particular officer is a stickler for getting the rules of grammar just right …

## 3. Kevin the Teenager

Parents of surly, uncooperative teens will recognise the change that led their charming offspring to transform into a scowling horror seemingly overnight when they watch this video. They'll empathise with Kevin's unfortunate parents. And if we're honest, most of us will have to admit that we were more than a little like Kevin at times …

## 4. Guide Cat

Anyone who is owned by a cat will know how absurd the thought of training a cat to help humans is. If anything, cats seem to spend their few waking moments plotting how to trip us up! So the idea of using a cat as an assistance animal is naturally rich in comedy material. The Guide Cat clearly doesn't care one bit about helping his hapless owner out …

## 5. Women - Don't Drive!

Public information films or public service announcements seem quaint today, with their dated notions of how people should behave. This spoof of PIFs sends up the 1940s ideal of femininity and the roles of women beautifully. You see, girls, our poor little female brains just aren't built to handle the complexities of the motor car, so we should leave the driving to men!

## 6. Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?

You'll need good Spanish to understand this one, so if you don't speak the language find a friend who does and ask them to translate for you. In this sketch, an unfortunate contestant in the Spanish version of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire? finds that he's presented with a question that's impossible to answer …

## 7. Henry VIII Online Dating

If you've not watched the UK series Horrible Histories, then you've got five hilarious seasons to catch up on. It's one of those series that's aimed at children but that people can enjoy whatever their age. In this sketch, the much-married English monarch Henry VIII checks out online dating to find himself a new wife. Yes, I know they didn't have computers in the 16th century …

As ever, it's hard to pick out some favorite sketches when there are so many brilliant skits available on YouTube. But these ones should make you laugh (unless you don't understand Spanish)! What is your favorite sketch of all time - the one that makes you laugh however many times you've seen it?
