# 7 Best Times to Be in a Relationship ...

Being single can be awesome, but you should recognize the best times to be in a relationship. It’s great to have someone that's always there for you, willing to help you out when you’re in a pickle. No matter what situation you get yourself into, they'll be ready to be your savior. Whether you're single or not, you can agree that these are the best times to be in a relationship:

## 1. When You See a Bug

When there’s a spider in your room, you decide that you never really wanted to live there anyway. You could kill it if you really had to, but no one wants to approach those big things. One of the best times to be in a relationship is when you need help in the bug department. You don’t have to go through the trouble of crushing it, or capturing it and releasing it outside, because your partner will do the dirty work for you.

## 2. When You’re Cold

There’s no need to check the weather before you go outside. If you want to wear a tank top in winter, go for it. If you want to wear your bathing suit in the snow, it’s okay. If you didn’t wear enough layers, your partner will offer their jacket to you. You never have to worry about being cold again.

## 3. When You’re Poor

If you’re running low on cash, you can tell your partner you want to go out for dinner, just the two of you. When the bill comes, they’ll offer to pay. You won’t have to worry about starving, because you can order a lobster dinner without paying a cent. It’s the beauty of being in a relationship.

## 4. When You’re Tired

It’s scary to fall asleep on a train or bus, but if you’re with your partner, you have nothing to worry about. They’ll protect you from any sort of danger. Plus, they’re the perfect pillow. Rest your head on them, and you’ll be able to fall asleep anywhere.

## 5. When You Want Friends

Entering a relationship provides you with a bunch of new friends... or enemies. Your partner’s buddies become your buddies. You may not hang out on a weekly basis, but you’re sure to see them often. If you all get along, then there you go--a whole new crew.

## 6. When You’re Confused

If you don’t understand a joke, it’s embarrassing to ask someone what it means. Of course, your partner’s not allowed to judge you. If you’re lost during a conversation or can’t figure out what a comedian’s trying to say, ask away. Your partner’s like your personal search engine.

## 7. When You Want New Clothes

Now, your partner might not have a closet full of the latest fashions, but they’ll be willing to share what they do have with you. If you need a new hoodie, ask them to borrow theirs. You don’t have to tell them your real reasoning. Lie and say you want it so you can smell their scent, even when you’re miles apart. In reality, you just want to expand your wardrobe, but they don’t need to know that.

Being in a relationship is awesome, for more reasons than you'd think. Are you currently dating someone? What’s the greatest perk of being with them?
