# Are You an Optimist or a Pessimist?

Two people can see the exact same situation in two completely different ways. That's because there are optimists in the world and pessimists in the world. These funny comics from [College Humor](https://bit.ly/1UQIF7c) can help you figure out which side of the fence you land on:

![Dating](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yc/my/xa1k7evl565dcbfdedc92039525519_600x416.jpg)

Do you get excited when you develop a crush, or is it horrible news?

## 2. College

![College](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/wx/jdxijrf7565dcbfe4f8b1067274246_600x416.jpg)
