# Send a Silly Quote about Friendship to Your Bestie ...

You love your bestie, which is why you should send her some silly quotes about friendship. She deserves to know how much you care about her, so don't let a day go by without reminding her how lucky you are to have her. A best friend is something special, and don't forget it! Here are a few silly quotes about friendship that describe your relationship perfectly:

## 1. Never Alone

![Never Alone](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u6/xv/guf8aasp_400x280.jpg)

When you have a dumb idea, your friend will either talk you out of it or go along with your plan. She isn't going to let you make a fool out of yourself alone. She wants to be right there by your side, because that's what friends are for. This is one of the silly quotes about friendship that anyone with a BFF can understand. It's just the way your relationship works.

## 2. Never Selfish

![Never Selfish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/jt/ymkrmm49_600x582.jpg)

You always want to be the most beautiful person in the room, which is why you wouldn't want your friend to buy a dress she looks gorgeous wearing. She'll outshine you, and you'll never get the attention you deserve. Of course, you love your friend so much that you'll give up your spot as hottest girl at the party. She deserves to be admired just as much as you do.

## 3. Never Apart

![Never Apart](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rv/lg/wxsgsbta_600x420.jpg)

No matter what gender you're attracted to, your friends and family are going to assume that you're in a relationship with your bestie. You're just so close that it appears like you're dating. Plus, you're comfortable with each other, so you're not afraid to hold hands and cuddle. There's nothing wrong with being extra affectionate.

## 4. Never Shy

![Never Shy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bb/ax/qk56ujhk_500x301.jpg)

Most of your friends will knock on the door when they arrive at your house and will politely wait for you to offer them a snack. But your bestie will barge right through the door and dig through your fridge. She knows you'll let her eat whatever she wants anyway, so why ask? She treats your house like hers, because she's basically a member of the family.

## 5. Never Mad

![Never Mad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jv/f3/ejcc33q9_500x261.jpg)

It's hard to stay angry with someone whom you talk to about every aspect of your life. You want to give her the silent treatment as a punishment, but you're also dying to tell her about the look your crush just gave you. You'll try to stay mad at her but it won't last long, because she's just too important to ignore.

## 6. Never Ashamed

![Never Ashamed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yo/ay/nazy518e_400x266.jpg)

Strangers may think you're pretty normal, but your friends know that you're absolutely insane. However, they won't let that stop them from hanging out with you. They love you too much to be embarrassed by all of the crazy things that you say and do.

## 7. Never Judged

![Never Judged](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/in/lx/x3ixg1j1_600x600.jpg)

A best friend will act like you're the greatest thing in the world. Sure, she'll insult you, but it'll all be in good fun. When she's not joking around, she'll admit that you're one of her favorite people in the entire world. Even though she knows all of your flaws, she still adores you.

Best friends are something special. They're someone you can laugh with, cry with, and go crazy with. Never forget how lucky you are to have someone so amazing in your life. How long have you known your best friend?
