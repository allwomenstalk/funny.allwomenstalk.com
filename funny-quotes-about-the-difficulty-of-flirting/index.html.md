# 7 Funny Quotes about the Difficulty of Flirting ...

Quotes about flirting will make you feel better about your awkward interactions. Sometimes, your crush will respond well to your compliments and will ask you on a date before you know it. Other times, he won't even realize that you're trying to get with him. Whether you're a professional flirt or just plain awkward, you should be able to relate to these quotes about flirting:

## 1. Friendly or Flirting

![Friendly or Flirting](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kx/v0/r708tcwd_580x415.jpg)

It's hard to define flirting. Some people will accuse you of flirting with your male friends when you're not even trying to be seductive. That's why it's easy to get away with saying that you weren't intentionally trying to land a date with your crush, even if you were. You can just take advice from one of these great quotes about flirting and claim that you were being extra friendly to someone extra attractive.

## 2. Like to Look

![Like to Look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wy/jr/o8lj59wg_600x420.jpg)

Eye contact is a huge part of flirting, but only if your crush notices it. You can't stare at him whenever he's turned the other way and expect him to magically know that you're interested. As long as you let him see you checking him out, you stand a chance. It's not as good as approaching him, but it's better than doing nothing.

## 3. Embrace Your Awkwardness

![Embrace Your Awkwardness](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oh/bd/ym8x7ta4_425x357.jpg)

Some of us aren't as skilled with the art of flirting as others are. If you always end up having strange interactions with your crush, don't worry. He might be happy that you're different than the other girls that are interested in him. Believe it or not, your awkwardness could pay off, so don't be ashamed of it.

## 4. Best Balance

If you're head over heels for your crush, you can't let him know that. You have to show him that you're interested, but not obsessed. It's hard to strike the right balance, but if you don't want to scare him off, you have to be cautious.

## 5. If Only

![If Only](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/x8/p7c9t553_500x433.jpg)

Some people prefer to flirt over the internet or over their phones, because it's easier than talking to their crush in person. That's why it would be nice if flirting face to face was more like a video game. Don't you wish you could just click a few buttons and make him fall for you?

## 6. Unashamed Stares

![Unashamed Stares](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/af/e3/gcxiv1r6_425x237.jpg)

You shouldn't be embarrassed about looking at attractive sights. It's human nature to admire beauty. If your crush is super sexy, then he should be used to women staring at him. That means you shouldn't be embarrassed if he notices your glances. It might even help him realize that you're interested and land you a date, so don't be afraid of being obvious.

## 7. All Mine

![All Mine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nl/s0/chtvkq7t_500x350.jpg)

Have you ever watched your crush flirt with another girl? Even though he has the right to do as he pleases, it's the worst feeling in the world. That's why you need to get to him before another girl does. Make sure you're the one he ends up calling his girlfriend.

Flirting can be difficult, time consuming, and confusing, but it can help you score a date with your crush. Approaching him is worth the potential embarrassment, so be brave and walk right up to him! Are you a good flirt or do you have trouble talking to guys that you like?
