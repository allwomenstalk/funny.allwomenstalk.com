# Girls with Anxiety Can Appreciate and Relate to These Comic Strips ...

Okay, these comics are funny, even thought they're about something that's not funny at all: anxiety. If you suffer from panic and anxiety of any kind, these comics were made just for you, so grab your favorite blanket and maybe a cup of tea, and see how many of these you can relate to.

## 1. Party Girl

![cartoon, font, play, brand, illustration,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/fd/oj9m0cwq57f3c81e14694164208483_600x600.jpg)

Oh my goodness, yes. Those are my three favorite things. Well, swap "tea" for "ice cream" and we're good.

## 2. Reward Stickers

![font, product, brand, line, ANXIETY,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/na/x5/ed1xa9s057f3c81e35f58156835829_600x562.jpg)

Today, I'd have earned the phone call one. Why oh why is it so hard to make calls?

## 3. So Real

![text, cartoon, font, biology, illustration,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ov/x3/obeoozhi57f3c81ebc071581709065_600x546.jpg)

This is EXACTLY how panic and anxiety feel... I get the sweaty hands and the wobbly knees.

## 4. Hands

![cartoon, font, play, clip art, illustration,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pw/ac/ozlpcgm757f3c81e87fcc582317428_600x588.jpg)

That's it. I'm carrying a hot dog with me at all times, and some mustard of course. "What do I do with my hands" problem solved.

## 5. Calming Thoughts

![cartoon, font, product, brand, line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/si/kq/e7sc184w57f3c81ea90a5480991167_600x589.jpg)

There's nothing cuter than daddy penguins. Google some images. Seriously. They need a meme or something, stat.

## 6. When You're Sad

![font, line, shape, diagram, WHAT,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u9/qb/otxx53wm57f3c81edac70361153580_600x590.jpg)

The nest thing is my healing. Which one of these is yours? Is there another way you make your soul feel better?

## 7. How It Feels

![text, cartoon, font, brand, line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sg/eg/irybsstg57f3c8216995d248649333_600x583.jpg)

Don't you wish more people understood this? Maybe we should make this comic required viewing in middle schools.

## 8. The opposite of Conversation Starters

![icon, font, emoticon, brand, line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eu/ea/awglyguj57f3c8212d45d566799158_600x585.jpg)

The "rude" and "honest opinion" ones usually mean someone's about to insult me, so I just walk away...

## 9. Scale

![font, line, diagram, shape, brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/av/e1/wlidsqa257f3c821d3a01871968839_600x584.jpg)

Handshake, high-five, or a HUG. I can't count how many times I've gone in for a hug and all they wanted was a handshake. I'm getting anxious just thinking about it...

Okay, so which of these comics did you relate to most? How do you cope with anxiety and panic?
