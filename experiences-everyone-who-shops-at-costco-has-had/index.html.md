# Experiences Everyone Who Shops at Costco Has Had ...

Having a Costco membership is special. I mean, it's not hard to get one if you're willing to pay for the privilege, so it's not completely exclusive or anything, it's just … well, the membership is incidental, really. It's simply the Golden Ticket you need to reach the wonder of Costco. And there, my friends, there waits a world of magical experiences.

## 1. The Promise of Free Samples

![The Promise of Free Samples](https://img.allw.mn/content/un/om/hvubom0c56e9c9057ff69484467881_500x282.gif)

They never stand a chance. You are willing to try everything because it costs nothing.

## 2. What It's like when Everyone else Has the Same Idea as You

![What It's like when Everyone else Has the Same Idea as You](https://img.allw.mn/content/lu/ye/gijbjpss56e9c905ec087239583530_500x255.gif)

You have to feel sorry for the people manning the sample tables. We attack like a school of sharks.

## 3. The Endless Lines on Weekends

![The Endless Lines on Weekends](https://img.allw.mn/content/ju/jy/ezkap11n56e9c9063572a520008377_500x226.gif)

So many people, so many overloaded carts, so many huge vats of things.

## 4. Fighting for Your Place among All the Other Shoppers in General

![Fighting for Your Place among All the Other Shoppers in General](https://img.allw.mn/content/cn/wg/cruddpb256e9c9069dbe0712563005_500x281.gif)

They're just everywhere – with every right to be there, of course, but that does not matter because this is your Costco.

## 5. Dealing with That One D-bag Who's Always Exactly Where You Want to Be

![Dealing with That One D-bag Who's Always Exactly Where You Want to Be](https://img.allw.mn/content/ep/a3/f9hudglu56e9c906afef3060291769_500x275.gif)

It's just … it's just that there's so much of Costco to get through. There simply is not time to navigate certain situations that impede your progress. One time I ran into a barricade of high chairs lined up in the aisle.

## 6. The Sheer, Awe-inspiring Size of the Snack Aisles

![The Sheer, Awe-inspiring Size of the Snack Aisles](https://img.allw.mn/content/e0/d7/gxf97nyj56e9c907259a6371876344_480x264.gif)

So many snacks in such ginormous quantities, it's like munchies heaven.

## 7. That Overwhelming Need to Buy an Overabundance of Everything

![That Overwhelming Need to Buy an Overabundance of Everything](https://img.allw.mn/content/et/qv/ddrnlfzz56e9c906a80c6449421186_500x260.gif)

Don't get me wrong, it's practical to buy huge packs of toilet paper, laundry detergent, and things like that, but that industrial-sized bag of Pizza Rolls is just next level. …actually, is that a real thing?

## 8. Trying to Dodge the Pushy Salespeople

![Trying to Dodge the Pushy Salespeople](https://img.allw.mn/content/z0/q5/xso37llt56e9c9074dbfc511808939_500x282.gif)

They are very aggressive. Like, very aggressive. That's annoying … until you happen to need whatever they're offering.

## 9. Gearing up to Get in and out as Quickly as Possible

![Gearing up to Get in and out as Quickly as Possible](https://img.allw.mn/content/si/lf/pxtrashn56e9c907bb121936056228_500x267.gif)

And the triumph when you clock in at less than half an hour!

## 10. When Someone else is All up in Your Aisle

Especially when it happens more than once. Because, again, this is your Costco.

## 11. Discovering the Booze Section for the First Time

![Discovering the Booze Section for the First Time](https://img.allw.mn/content/h1/sh/r0n0yhvi56e9c908dba10426463469_500x250.gif)

There's nothing like it. All that liquor. Such huge quantities.

## 12. Getting Stuck Listening to a Vitamin Pitch

![Getting Stuck Listening to a Vitamin Pitch](https://img.allw.mn/content/pd/py/qh0htj9k56e9c908587c4208882651_500x289.gif)

Nooooo!

## 13. The Willa Wonka Wonderland That is the Candy Aisle

![The Willa Wonka Wonderland That is the Candy Aisle](https://img.allw.mn/content/l0/lx/kuqw2m3956e9c908b975e548444983_500x281.gif)

I can't even think about it without getting the tingles, tbh.

## 14. Trying to Follow a Massive Costco List via Text

![Trying to Follow a Massive Costco List via Text](https://img.allw.mn/content/mf/s8/ldrify5256e9c908a668d063471054_500x250.gif)

It's worse if you write it down, though. Keeping up with paper in Costco is difficult.

## 15. The First Time You Run into That One Couple

![The First Time You Run into That One Couple](https://img.allw.mn/content/aw/p1/mlpaul9956e9c909a02c0409804870_500x300.gif)

You know. The couple who's more than happy to, ahem, try out the chairs or futons or mattresses.

## 16. Realizing How Much Money You Actually Spent

[https://media.giphy.com/media/11oB8BlbNUI372/giphy.gif](https://media.giphy.com/media/11oB8BlbNUI372/giphy.gif)
It's always so much. So much money.

## 17. But You Know You're Always Prepared for the Zombie Apocalypse

![But You Know You're Always Prepared for the Zombie Apocalypse](https://img.allw.mn/content/yd/sv/o9h40j0056e9c9095a5ad336433009_500x273.gif)

Costco turns all of us into preppers.

And don't even get me started on trying Costco food for the first time. What's your favorite big-big-big box store experience?
