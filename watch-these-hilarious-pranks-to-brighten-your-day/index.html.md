# Watch These Hilarious Pranks to Brighten Your Day ...

These hilarious prank videos will be sure to brighten your day after a bad day at work! These are just a few pranks by New Zealand comedians Jono and Ben (who have heaps of laughs on their TV show) so join in on the classic New Zealand sense of humour and watch all of these funny pranks unravel over just a short couple of minutes each! 😄 ✌️ ❤️ ✨

## 1. Office Fumigation Prank ⚠️ 💀 🐜

In this hilarious video, Jono and Ben prank their boss Brad who is coming into work one morning to find a fumigation for ants taking place inside. Brad rushes in to grab things from his office but gets trapped inside, just as smoke starts to fill the room and he starts freaking out - totally thinking the whole thing is real!

## 2. Supermarket Checkout Prank 🍒 🍎 🍌

'Sione's Wedding' actor Robbie Magasiva pretends to be working in a local supermarket and is told to prank each customer as they come through the checkout. He pretends to be a robot, tries to fit all the purchases into one overflowing shopping bags, eat their food and takes the money for himself!

## 3. McDonalds Order Prank 🍟 🍷 🍔

Actor and singer Stan Walker pretends to be taking orders at McDonalds, where he licks a customer's hamburger, disapproves of orders that are given to him and puts fries up his nose when serving a customer!

## 4. Store Mannequin Prank 🚪 🏃 💃

Jono and Ben pretend to be mannequins in a popular clothing store, where they stand completely still by the entrance and jump out at customers walking in! Many of them get a real fright and run out of the store.

## 5. Hardware Store Prank 🔨 🔫 🔧

In this hilarious prank, actor Tammy Davis pretends to be working at hardware store 'Mitre 10' and is tells a customer that their spades are great for digging graves, swears as much as he can when trying to sell a product and encourages a woman to grow a certain kind of illegal plant in her vege garden!

## 6. Fake News Prank 🌏 🎥 🎤

This hilarious prank shows news presenter Kate Hawkesby interviewing random people on the street, telling them she wants their opinion on the weather. However when they are being interviewed, Kate changes the interview topic to see who's willing to play along with it!

## 7. London Fake News (part 1) ♕ 🏰 💂

Olivia Lee takes on London when she interviews people on the street, once again asking them outrageous questions on camera, including one older woman's obsession with Prince Harry and another who supposedly hates baby George!

## 8. London Fake News (part 2) 👸🏻 🏰 ♛

In the second part of London's fake news, Olivia Lee continues her questioning of strangers on the streets, including one man's struggle of being a 70 year old virgin and a romantic couple who are actually siblings!
