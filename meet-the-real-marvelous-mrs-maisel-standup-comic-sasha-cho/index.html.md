# Meet the Real Marvelous Mrs. Maisel \- Standup Comic Sasha Cho ...

Are you a fan of Midge Maisel? Are you too glued to the phone screen watching every single episode of the new season? Then we have great news for you!

If you ever find yourself in the fabulous NYC, you may actually find yourself at a standup of the real-life Mrs. Maisel.

Young, gorgeous, hot, Jewish and separated, Sasha Cho (real name - Alexandra Chorna) lives in New York City

and is killing it on a regular basis in the hottest standup comedy clubs in Downtown.

The similarities between Mrs. Maisel and Sasha are mind-blowing, see for yourself:

## 1. How Did You Start Your Standup Career?

Oh. It’s almost as if the whole world has seen me do it. I got into an argument with my husband. I got drunk. I ended up at a local bar with an open mic. I went on stage. And that is how my life changed.

## 2. Did You Ever Consider Doing Standup before?

Honestly, I always liked it. But I never thought it could be an opportunity for me. You know, having two little kids, taking care of them full time.

## 3. Do You Remember Anything from Your Original Set?

Of course. A lot of jokes have evolved and grown. But I still cherish that spontaneous outburst. I’ve heard that someone taped it, but I never found the person. It would be great to see it.

## 4. Is Standup Your Main Job?

No. I actually have a day job. I’m a fashion stylist. I specialize in male fashion.

## 5. Have You Heard of Marvelous Mrs. Maisel?

I did. A friend of mine kept on telling me about the show. She insisted that I watch it. And so I did.

## 6. Do You See the Similarities between You and Midge?

Hard to deny, actually. We’re both Jewish. And I must say that my brisket is most definitely better, as are my jokes. It’s not like there’s a competition. But if there was... *laughs*

I have 2 children and a complicated marriage situation too. Of course, Michael Zegen is much prettier. So Midge gets a point there.

*note from the editor - Sasha is a knock-out, as you can see from the top photo, so one more similarity with the onscreen personality right there)

## 7. Where Can We See You Perform?

In comedy clubs all over New York. I try to announce my shows in my Instagram stories.

Thank you Sasha for the amazing interview and for the many laughs, god knows, we, women need that!

Take it from us, if you are in NYC, you definitely want to come see her perform on stage, follow Sasha on Instagram - [@MadMamaNYC](https://www.facebook.com/unsupportedbrowser) c - to know all about her upcoming standup performances.
