# 17 Responses to Someone Asking Whats Wrong  ...

Don't you hate when you're just sitting around, minding your own business, and someone walks up to you and asks what's wrong. If you're actually in a good mood that day, you'll be insulted. Even if they hit the nail on the head and you're genuinely upset, you probably won't want to talk about it. So here are a few responses to use the next time someone asks you what's wrong:

## 1. The Fact That I Have Resting Bitch Face

![The Fact That I Have Resting Bitch Face](https://img.allw.mn/content/s2/hq/uok389ht57051bf041129491415261_500x280.gif)

Even if you're happier than you've ever been, people will still ask what's wrong, because you naturally look miserable. It's not your fault. It's just your face.

## 2. Do You Want a List?

![Do You Want a List?](https://img.allw.mn/content/fe/cz/ubcslwgh57051bef90496431819375_500x252.gif)

There isn't one single thing bothering you at any given time. There's usually a long list of things that they won't actually want to hear about.

## 3. There’s Trouble Back on My Home Planet

![There’s Trouble Back on My Home Planet](https://img.allw.mn/content/bt/jw/hqc01dew57051bf07f56a671651000_480x272.gif)

Hopefully, this will weird them out enough to walk away.

## 4. You Should Know

![You Should Know](https://img.allw.mn/content/ch/ot/n3uznfqp57051bf116e21367487982_480x270.gif)

Make it sound like it's all their fault. That'll confuse the hell out of them.

## 5. How Much I Care

![How Much I Care](https://img.allw.mn/content/pq/z4/qq6d65eq57051bf035136565704611_500x234.gif)

This is a pretty honest answer. If you didn't care about things so much, you wouldn't be so stressed.

## 6. I Can’t Even Explain That to Myself

![I Can’t Even Explain That to Myself](https://img.allw.mn/content/mk/ti/wap6ci3y57051bf27ec1a445645636_500x281.gif)

If you can't figure out why you're upset, how could you explain it to someone else who doesn't understand your situation?

## 7. You’re Talking to Me when I Could Be Watching Netflix

![You’re Talking to Me when I Could Be Watching Netflix](https://img.allw.mn/content/ka/wa/ogdph17r57051bf14d2d5863601029_500x281.gif)

Anytime you're away from Netflix is a bad time.

## 8. I’m Not a Cat, so I Can’t Lay around All Day

![I’m Not a Cat, so I Can’t Lay around All Day](https://img.allw.mn/content/qo/m9/e7yliftk57051bf513ceb333937843_500x266.gif)

They're the luckiest animal. They get to do what they want when they want, and all they want is to lounge around.

## 9. That People Keep Asking Me That Question

![color, hair, facial expression, face, image,](https://img.allw.mn/content/oz/ic/lgvn8f3e58d101a2698f0936288962_500x275.gif)

It's frustrating to be asked the same thing over and over again. It can drive anybody nuts.

## 10. There Aren’t Enough Hours in a Day to Answer That Question

![There Aren’t Enough Hours in a Day to Answer That Question](https://img.allw.mn/content/aq/hd/h2mqh4gj57051bf22ec50196023951_500x250.gif)

Even if there were, no one would sit around for long enough to listen to your entire rant.

## 11. I’m Sexually Frustrated. Wanna Fix It?

![I’m Sexually Frustrated. Wanna Fix It?](https://img.allw.mn/content/he/al/l3eu6pxm57051bf2ad895698914658_500x227.gif)

You might as well try to get some sex out of the deal.

## 12. Kanye Isn’t Returning My Texts

![person, mouth, sense, interaction,](https://img.allw.mn/content/yj/vi/cvwzp0zr58d101a2afff0300033895_500x284.gif)

He thinks he's all high and mighty, just because he's famous.

## 13. Nothing You Could Help Me with

![Nothing You Could Help Me with](https://img.allw.mn/content/nk/by/yi1qgbq757051bf3a686d530967607_500x195.gif)

This is short, sweet, and to the point. Since they can't help you, hopefully they'll leave you alone.

## 14. People like You

![People like You](https://img.allw.mn/content/yi/b0/xvsg4s3a57051bf3b099f875876292_500x227.gif)

If you don't think they actually give a damn about your feelings, then you can use this line to put them in their place. The world would be better without fake people like them.

## 15. I Can’t Feel My Face when I’m with You

![I Can’t Feel My Face when I’m with You](https://img.allw.mn/content/cw/cv/x55xhsme57051bf421ef1233427414_500x330.gif)

When in doubt, just quote a popular song. The Weeknd is hot at the moment, so why not follow in his footsteps?

## 16. I’m Trying to Figure out How to Tell You the News...

![I’m Trying to Figure out How to Tell You the News...](https://img.allw.mn/content/x4/q4/z4bo448w57051bf41ceda212426372_400x226.gif)

If you want to freak them out, then you can use this line.

## 17. The Way You’re Looking at Me

![The Way You’re Looking at Me](https://img.allw.mn/content/o9/qh/gaedxe4057051bf518d86665331110_500x264.gif)

You don't want anyone's pity. In fact, that's the last thing that you want.

Now you'll be able to put nosy people in their place. What do you usually say when someone asks you what's wrong?
