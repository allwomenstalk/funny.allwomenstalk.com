# 7 French Pick\-up Lines That Are as Corny as They Are Romantic ...

If you're planning on traveling to Paris, or just want to impress a hottie in your foreign language class, there are French pick-up lines that you should try using. It is one of the most romantic languages, after all. How could you go wrong? Even when you're saying phrases that are completely ridiculous, they'll sound sweet to the unknowing ear. That's why you should try using these funny French pick-up lines:

## 1. Do You Have a Map? I'm Lost in Your Eyes

"Est-ce que tu as un plan? Je me suis perdu dans tes yeux." This is one of the French pick-up lines that'll work well if you're actually lost in a new city. Of course, you can always use it when the GPS on your iPhone is working perfectly fine. All that matters is that it allows you to talk to a cutie with amazing eyes.

## 2. Can I Call You Biscuit? Because I Find You Irresistible

"Puis-je t'appeler biscotte? Parce que je te trouve craquante." If you enjoy eating biscuits, then this just might work for you. I mean, who doesn't adore the delicious treat? It's a bizarre thing to be compared to, but it's still a compliment.

## 3. Is It Hot in Here, or is It Just You?

"Il fait chaud ici, ou c'est juste toi?" This is a classic, but it sounds better in French than English. If you're saying it to someone who doesn't understand the language, you can lie and say that you've just uttered something impressive and oh so romantic. They'll never know the difference--as long as you keep them away from Google Translate.

## 4. I Just Arrived in This City. Could You Tell Me the Way to Your Apartment?

"Je viens d'arriver dans ta ville. Est-ce que tu pourrais m'indiquer le chemin jusqu'à ton appartement?" This question takes an unexpected turn, which will hopefully make the object of your affection laugh. If you're lucky, he'll even give you his address. Just be careful if you don't know him, because we all know about stranger danger.

## 5. Hi, I Just Realized This but You Look a Lot like My Next Boyfriend

"J'ai juste realisé ceci, mais vous ressemblez beaucoup à mon prochain petit ami." This one will make you appear confident. You're not bothering to ask him out, because you already know that he'll be your boyfriend in the near future. Now all you have to do is sit back and wait.

## 6. Would You like to Talk, or Should I Keep Ogling You from a Distance?

"Voulez vous parler, ou bien dois-je continuer à vous reluquer de lá oú je suis?" Make sure you say this to someone who seems to be returning your glances. If they're not interested, this remark could make you seem like a stalker.

## 7. What Are You Eating to Be so Beautiful?

"Qu’est-ce que tu manges pour être beau de même?" This one doesn't make much sense, but hey, the word 'beautiful' is in it, so it must be good. If you're brave, go with it. Hopefully your crush will be happy about it, and that's all that matters.

Although you can say some pretty funny things in French, it's a beautiful language that can be utterly romantic. Are you fluent in French? What's the most romantic thing that you can say in the language?
