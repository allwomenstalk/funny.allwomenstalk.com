# 15 Clever Disney Related Pick up Lines ...

If you want to seduce someone with Disney references, there are some hilarious Disney related pick-up lines out there. They might not get you a date, but they'll at least get you a few laughs. That's better than nothing, isn't it? Here are some of the best Disney related pick-up lines for you to enjoy:

## 1. Monsters Inc

![blue,screenshot,fictional character,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/of/i9/in/psnk0_blue_screenshot_fictional-character_600x450.jpg)

“My name isn't Sully, but you could be my Boo.” This is one of the greatest Disney related pick-up lines, because it's cute and to the point. There's no crude humor in it, and it lets your crush know that you have great taste in film. I mean, who doesn't love Monsters Inc.?

## 2. Beauty and the Beast

![cartoon,anime,screenshot,fictional character,illustration,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aw/cv/xj/f4wmo_cartoon_anime_screenshot_fictional-character_illustration_600x450.jpg)

“Am I a Beast, because you’re definitely a beauty?” This is a perfect twist on traditional compliments. Instead of telling someone they're pretty, use this on them. It's more original.

## 3. Winnie the Pooh

![Downtown Disney,cartoon,play,jungle,screenshot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/us/rw/otmde_downtown-disney_cartoon_play_jungle_screenshot_600x450.jpg)

“Call me Pooh, because all I want is you, honey.” Short and sweet. What could be better?

## 4. Cinderella

![Cinderella (1950),Cinderella,Cinderella,Cinderella,Pinocchio 1940 VHS,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oq/xa/j1/mvaqc_cinderella-1950_cinderella_cinderella_cinderella_pinocchio-1940-vhs_600x450.jpg)

“You must be Cinderella, because I see that dress disappearing by midnight.” This one is only for the brave! Use it with caution. You don't want a drink thrown in your face.

## 5. Cars

![Cars,CARS,car,vehicle,automotive design,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dq/p0/v8/rzmkc_cars_cars_car_vehicle_automotive-design_600x480.jpg)

“I must be Lighting McQueen, 'cause you've got my heart racing.” This is the Disney alternative of saying "you've been running through my mind all day."

## 6. Sleeping Beauty

![cartoon,anime,screenshot,illustration,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h7/b5/ht/vbn6r_cartoon_anime_screenshot_illustration_600x438.jpg)

“After meeting me, you'll want to spend more time in bed than Sleeping Beauty.” This is another one for the boldest of the bold. Only use it with people that are able to appreciate the joke.

## 7. Toy Story

![TOY STORY 2,play,toy,screenshot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q8/yd/uy/yr2cc_toy-story-2_play_toy_screenshot_600x480.jpg)

“I can take you to infinity and beyond.” Everybody, and I mean everybody, loves Toy Story. This one can't go wrong.

## 8. Aladdin

![cartoon,anime,screenshot,comics,illustration,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/we/u2/fx/03ps1_cartoon_anime_screenshot_comics_illustration_600x462.jpg)

“One night with me, and I'll show you a whole new world.” If you're really aiming to impress, start singing the song that goes along with it. What could go wrong?

## 9. Lion King

![mammal,natural environment,ecosystem,cartoon,lion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v5/ie/a6/pme0q_mammal_natural-environment_ecosystem_cartoon_lion_600x450.jpg)

“Can you feel the love tonight?” This is a nice, little question to ask any Lion King fan. If you're lucky, they'll say 'yes.'

## 10. Amusement Park

![Disneyland, Sleeping Beauty Castle,Disneyland,Disneyland,amusement park,walt disney world,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qg/0a/05/d4btf_disneyland-sleeping-beauty-castle_disneyland_disneyland_amusement-park_walt-disney-world_600x400.jpg)

“There are people who say Disneyland is the happiest place on earth. Apparently, none of them have ever been in your arms.” This isn’t your typical pick-up line, because it’s more adorable than raunchy. Say this to someone, and they won’t be able to hide their smile.

## 11. Alice in Wonderland

![cartoon,play,illustration,child art,comics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/ko/08/ac1h1_cartoon_play_illustration_child-art_comics_600x450.jpg)

“Is your name Alice? Because I’m lost in your Wonderland.” This is another clever way to compliment one's appearance without using typical compliments. No one would forget hearing this!

## 12. Mickey and Minnie

![cartoon,anime,mouth,mangaka,organ,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n9/i9/x5/m0h6v_cartoon_anime_mouth_mangaka_organ_600x428.jpg)

“Hey my name's Mickey and there's nothing Minnie about me.” Only say this if it's true. No need to lie.

## 13. Goofy

![Goofy Games,horse,cartoon,mammal,horse like mammal,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lj/ry/x0/qawt0_goofy-games_horse_cartoon_mammal_horse-like-mammal_600x450.jpg)

“Do you wanna get Goofy?” Be careful with this one, because it's not obvious that it's a Disney reference. Save it for when you're already in the middle of a Disney centered conversation.

## 14. Tower of Terror

![Disney World, The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror,landmark,historic site,building,town,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mr/vz/bl/idu4x_disney-world-the-twilight-zone-tower-of-terror_landmark_historic-site_building_town_600x450.jpg)

“I don’t need to take you on the Tower of Terror to get you screaming.” Just make sure they know that the 'screaming' is positive, and not a threat.

## 15. Snow White

![cartoon,person,play,anime,screenshot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fl/rr/nz/j6a6o_cartoon_person_play_anime_screenshot_600x470.jpg)

“I’m the eighth dwarf--Sexy.”

Which one of these pick-up lines do you like the best? Do you have any others that weren't mentioned?
