# 7 Funny Websites That Will Always Get You Laughing ...

When you’re having a bad day, you should head to funny websites to get a smile back on your face. The internet is full of so many random things that it can be difficult to find the great stuff. In order to save some time, go to the following funny websites:

## 1. College Humor

![College Humor](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mc/gs/kii9y4oc_600x568.jpg)

This is one of the funny websites that’s perfect for college aged students, but others will love it as well. As long as you’re mature enough for jokes with adult content, you’ll enjoy the articles, photos, and videos. If you’re a comedian, you can even submit content yourself. Who knows? You might create the next viral video.

## 2. Funny or Die

![Funny or Die](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/x1/ik21jcs1_600x471.jpg)

The best part of this website is that celebrities frequently contribute to it. Stars like James Franco and Zach Galifianakis have returned again and again to make viewers laugh. If you want to be instantly hooked, try watching the series called “Between Two Ferns,” which depicts Zach interviewing other celebrities in the funniest way possible. Or, if you’d rather start with something from Franco, watch his fake ad for Gucci.

## 3. Reddit

![Reddit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hi/od/an5038c6_600x387.jpg)

Anything you could possibly want is available on Reddit. There are plenty of subsections to choose from. If you want to go on there for news, you can do so. If you want to go there to gush about your favorite show, you can. Most importantly, if you want to find funny pictures and posts, you can! The website is littered with them, so you should find ways to laugh in no time.

## 4. BuzzFeed

![BuzzFeed](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uz/os/mb0l3duw_600x404.jpg)

This website consists of articles and videos. There are different sections like ‘LOL,’ ‘win,’ ‘omg,’ and ‘cute.’ Pick whatever category you’re interested in, and start reading. There’s so much material on this website that you’ll never run out of things to read. It’s the perfect entertainment.

## 5. Tumblr

![Tumblr](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f2/gs/fjyqc711_462x600.jpg)

If you make an account on Tumblr, you’ll be able to find tons of awesome blogs to follow. Your dashboard will only contain the material you like, so you can weed out the bad stuff. Tumblr is the perfect place to find funny items, because you’re pretty much in charge of what you see. If you don’t like something a blog posts, then all you have to do is unfollow them. It’s as simple as a click.

## 6. Cracked

![Cracked](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k6/ah/hnty0uge_500x359.jpg)

Cracked is a humorous website that talks a lot about movies, video games, and music. If you're interested in popular culture, then you'll love scrolling down these pages. It's another website that allows you to submit content, so you're always free to be part of the party. Why not give it a try, and create something to submit? You've got nothing to lose.

## 7. Spark Notes

![Spark Notes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i7/cw/wdz1yr16_400x320.jpg)

This website isn’t only for helping you with homework. There’s actually some pretty funny stuff on it, especially if you’re still in school. They make fun of common teenage problems, and poke fun at our favorite celebrities. If you have some free time, you might as well check it out.

Your friends aren't always around to provide you with laughter. When you're all alone, you might as well use your internet to its advantage. Head to one of these websites, and waste away your day. What website do you head to when you want a laugh?
