# 7 Truly Bizarre and Unexpected Finds You Can Actually Buy on Etsy

Sometimes, amongst all of the vintage objects and handmade treasures, there are some truly bizarre and unexpected finds on Etsy. These are things that make you think, “wait, did somebody actually think this was a good idea!?” Whether you are looking for a conversation starter or just want to see how creative (and sometimes questionable) people can be, these items are guaranteed to get a reaction. I hope you enjoy browsing these weird finds on Etsy!

## 1. Tooth Knuckle Ring

![Tooth Knuckle Ring](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wt/n2/g4569d3u_450x253.jpg)

Kicking off my list of weird finds on Etsy is this truly insane knuckle ring. Are those teeth real, you ask? Indeed, they are. Each ring is custom-made, and the teeth are sourced from friends, family, and even anonymous donors. If you are interested, you might want to start a dedicated savings fund, because this piece is priced at a whopping US$945. You can view the listing here: [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com).

## 2. Moose Poop Necklace

![Moose Poop Necklace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ie/f8/nabem033_480x640.jpg)

Yes, you read that correctly. This necklace is crafted from nine pieces of genuine moose waste that have been meticulously dried and coated in a protective layer of polyurethane. Rest assured, the seller claims there is "no smell"—thank goodness for that! Whether you would actually wear a necklace made of moose deposits is a whole other question. If you are feeling brave, you can find it for just under US$40 here: [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com).

## 3. Fetus Soap

![Foetus Soap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kd/ru/m7dcyocl_570x378.jpg)

Just in case you ever felt like washing yourself with something a bit more conceptual, here is a hand-crafted fetus-shaped soap. The artist intends for the piece to invite users to think deeply about unconventional ideas. If that sounds like something you want to add to your bathroom routine, you can find this unique soap for US$15 right here: [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com).

## 4. Turkey Tortoise Cozy

![Turkey Tortoise Cozy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d3/tx/x6y27gm7_570x407.jpg)

Your pet tortoise can become the center of attention with this surprisingly detailed turkey-themed cozy. As ridiculous as this may seem, it is hard to deny that it is a hilarious look for a pet. I am still left with the burning question: why do you want your tortoise to look like a holiday roast? If you want to get into the festive spirit early, you can pick up this gem for US$23 right here: [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com).

## 5. Pizza, Bacon and Dill Pickle Lip Balm

![Pizza, Bacon and Dill Pickle Lip Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mb/h9/ap9x5ugj_570x497.jpg)

Have you ever wondered what your favorite savory snacks would feel like on your lips? Well, this trio of lip balms is flavored like pizza, bacon, and pickles. While the bacon flavor is reportedly their bestseller, I have my doubts about whether it is as satisfying as the real thing. For just under US$8, this set is a total steal for those who love novelty items. Get yours here: [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com).

## 6. Scented Poo Candle

![Scented Poo Candle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dj/ox/lvbu5zt0_570x592.jpg)

Before you panic, let me clarify: this candle does not smell like actual waste—it smells like chocolate. However, one has to wonder if a candle shaped like this is really the best choice for home decor. If you are not yet sold, it even comes with its own tiny portable piece of turf. Your guess is as good as mine as to why! You can pick it up for US$15 here: [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com).

## 7. Toilet Paper Earrings

![Toilet Paper Earrings](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x9/ro/mu02v52d_570x570.jpg)

Whether you want to be prepared for anything or just really, really love the humble bathroom essential, you can now wear your passion. The listing claims that “security is always having plenty of toilet paper,” and apparently, that applies to jewelry too. You can pick up this pair of toilet paper-shaped earrings—made from beads and sterling silver—for a little over US$40 right here: [etsy.com](https://www.etsy.com).
