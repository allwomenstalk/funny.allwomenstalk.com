# 7 Free, Funny Online Games to Play Right Away ...

The Internet is filled with random things to keep you occupied when you're bored. However, you need to know where to look to find things that will entertain you. If you're sick of visiting the same old websites day in and day out, you should try something new for a change. Here are a few free, funny online games to play right away:

## 1. Office Slacking

[Y8.com](https://www.y8.com/games/office_slacking) will give you the most accurate representation of working at an office or of going to class. In it, you play a woman behind a computer who spends her time painting her nails and emailing her friends. Of course, she has to make sure her boss doesn't catch her doing all of these things, and that's where the hard part comes in. You need to complete mini games before her boss catches a glimpse of her slacking.

## 2. Student Simulator

If you've ever dreamed about going to college, _mousebreaker.com_ will help you feel like you're already there. You start by typing in your name, giving yourself personality traits, and choosing a degree. Then you get to choose what kind of friends you have, how much you'll study, and what you'll eat. It's as close to the real life college experience as you'll ever get.

## 3. Idiot Test

It's funny to watch others play [onemorelevel.com](http://www.onemorelevel.com/game/the_idiot_test), but it's a bit frustrating when you play it yourself. All you have to do is click a few buttons, but it's a lot harder than it looks. Give it a try to test just how smart you are. Once you're done, call over your parents or a sibling to see if they can beat your score.

## 4. Zombie Survival

[Onemorelevel.com](http://www.onemorelevel.com/game/zombie_survival_quiz) isn't exactly a game--it's a quiz that will ask you some pretty in depth questions about what you'd do during an apocalypse. It's the most intense zombie quiz you'll take, so try not to laugh too hard at the questions. Your answers will determine if you survive, after all.

## 5. Meal or No Meal

If you're a fan of Howie Mandel and his Deal or No Deal game, you'll love playing [flashgames247.com](https://goo.gl/3tt57F). It has the same idea as the game show, except you're revealing meals instead of numbers. It's a pretty funny twist on a great game. However, you might end up hungry after playing it, so make sure you have a snack nearby.

## 6. Cat Vs Dog

Everyone has a strong stance on whether dogs or cats are superior. If you've ever had a fight about it with a friend, you'll love playing [flashgames247.com](https://goo.gl/qUFyab). It shows a kitty and puppy on a beach, throwing seashells at each other. There's no better way to find out who's the alpha dog (or cat), so make your pet proud.

## 7. Office Lover Kiss

This game is programmed in a language other than English, but to begin playing [flashgames247.com](https://goo.gl/CjPYyw), all you have to do is click the button on the bottom righthand side. Then you need to click the screen to kiss your boyfriend. Just be careful, because you can't indulge in any PDA when your boss is looking. If he catches you, you'll be in major trouble.

The Internet contains some pretty ridiculous things, but hey, at least they keep us entertained. It's impossible to be bored when you have an Internet connection. What other free online games do you spend your time playing?
