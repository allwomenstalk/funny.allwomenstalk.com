# Funny Comebacks to Catcalls ...

It's rude and disrespectful for a man to yell at a woman on the street. That's why [Bustle](https://www.bustle.com/articles/123256-6-witty-responses-to-catcalling-even-though-you-dont-have-to-respond-if-you-dont-want) has created some comebacks that you can use in the future. Here they are:

!["You Couldn't Afford Me."](https://img.allw.mn/content/nk/rg/rjnat46w5644ca1ff1e59138265256_245x160.gif)

## 3. "do You Kiss Your Mother with That Mouth?"

## 4. "Does That Ever Work?"

!["Does That Ever Work?"](https://img.allw.mn/content/mx/jc/md510f0g5644ca2089070174353190_294x233.gif)

## 5. If Someone Tells You "Nice Tits" or "You're Beautiful," Just Say "I Know."

![If Someone Tells You "Nice Tits" or "You're Beautiful," Just Say "I Know."](https://img.allw.mn/content/o1/sb/yz17sppc5644ca20e1f89726249486_400x300.gif)
